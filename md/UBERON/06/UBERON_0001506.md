
## Class : brachialis muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001506
 * *Label* = brachialis muscle
 * *Definition* = The brachialis (brachialis anticus) is a muscle in the upper arm that flexes the elbow joint. It lies just deep of the biceps brachii, and is a synergist that assists the biceps brachii in flexing at the elbow. It makes up part of the floor of the region known as the cubital fossa.
