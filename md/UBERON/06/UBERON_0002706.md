
## Class : posterior nucleus of hypothalamus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002706
 * *Label* = posterior nucleus of hypothalamus
 * *Definition* = The posterior nucleus of the hypothalamus is one of the many nuclei that make up the hypothalamic region of the brain. Its function is thermoregulation (heating) of the body. Damage or destruction of this nucleus causes hypothermia.
