
## Class : kinethmoid bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001406
 * *Label* = kinethmoid bone
 * *Definition* = Sesamoid bone located in the premaxillary-ethmoid ligament, between the mesethmoid and the premaxilla. The kinethmoid is an unpaired median bone.
