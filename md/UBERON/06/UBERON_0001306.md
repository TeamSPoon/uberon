
## Class : cumulus oophorus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001306
 * *Label* = cumulus oophorus
 * *Definition* = A cluster of cells that project into the cavity of the  mature ovarian follicle. It is released with the embedded oocyte during ovulation. In order for fertilization to occur this layer must be penetrated by the spermatocyte. [WP,modified].
