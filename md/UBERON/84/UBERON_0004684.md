
## Class : raphe nuclei

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004684
 * *Label* = raphe nuclei
 * *Definition* = The raphe nuclei are thin plates of cells in and immediately adjacent to the sagittal plane. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = NIF_GrossAnatomy:nlx_anat_20090205, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = https://sourceforge.net/tracker/?func=detail&aid=3248146&group_id=36855&atid=440764 ]
 * *Comment* = we treat NR obscurus, NR magnus etc as parts of the raphe nuclei. This is consistent with treatment in FMA, where the class is 'set of raphe nuclei'
