
## Class : raphe nuclei

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004684
 * *Label* = raphe nuclei
 * *Definition* = The raphe nuclei are thin plates of cells in and immediately adjacent to the sagittal plane.
