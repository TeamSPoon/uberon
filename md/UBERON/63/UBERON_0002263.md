
## Class : lentiform nucleus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002263
 * *Label* = lentiform nucleus
 * *Definition* = The lentiform nucleus or lenticular nucleus comprises the putamen and the globus pallidus within the basal ganglia. It is a large, cone-shaped mass of gray matter just lateral to the internal capsule. [WP,unvetted].
