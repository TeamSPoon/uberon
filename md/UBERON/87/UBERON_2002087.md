
## Class : pectoral axillary process

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002087
 * *Label* = pectoral axillary process
 * *Definition* = Pectoral axillary process is an elongate structure formed by one or more additional postcleithra or modified scales or a combination of both, that is firmly attached to a skin fold above the articular condyle of the uppermost pectoral fin ray or lepidotrichium 1.
