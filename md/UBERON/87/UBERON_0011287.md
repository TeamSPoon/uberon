
## Class : rostral organ

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011287
 * *Label* = rostral organ
 * *Definition* = A putative electroreceptor organ located in the medial rostral cavity in the ethmoid portion of the chondrocranium of Coelacanths.
