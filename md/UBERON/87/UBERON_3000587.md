
## Class : obsolete supraangular

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000587
 * *Label* = obsolete supraangular
 * *Definition* = The suprangular or surangular is a jaw bone found in most land vertebrates, except mammals. It's usually in the back of the jaw, on the upper edge, and is connected to all other jaw bones: dentary, angular, splenial and articular. It is often a muscle attachment site.
