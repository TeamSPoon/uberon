
## Class : sphenotic

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000587
 * *Label* = sphenotic
 * *Definition* = Endochondral bone that sutures with the pterosphenoid anteriorly, the prootic ventrally, the frontal bone dorso-medially, and the pterotic dorso-posteriorly. Occasionally the sphenotic sutures with the parietal bone. The sphenotic separates the orbital from the otic region. The sphenotic bears a large antero-lateral or lateral or ventro-lateral process that borders the postero-dorsal margin of the orbit. The sphenotic is a paired bone. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = ZFIN:curator ]
 * *Comment* = The lower edge of the sphenotic may contribute to the hyomandibular facet depending on the fish group.
