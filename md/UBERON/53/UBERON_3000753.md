
## Class : pars glenoidalis of scapula

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000753
 * *Label* = pars glenoidalis of scapula
 * *Definition* = Concave structure (posteromedial head) that forms the posterior head of the ventral margin of the scapula. The pars glenoidalis articulates with the humerus, and comprises the dorsal portion of the glenoid fossa. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + AAO:LAP]
