
## Class : orbicularis oris muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010933
 * *Label* = orbicularis oris muscle
 * *Definition* = The orbicularis oris is a superficial facial muscle with fibers that encircle the opening of the oral cavity that attaches to the maxilla, mandible, skin and modiolus, and that participates in oral/pharyngeal behaviors, and is innervated by the facial nerve (CN VII).
