
## Class : diencephalon

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001894
 * *Label* = diencephalon
 * *Definition* = Organ component of neuraxis that has as its parts the epithalamus, thalamus, hypothalamus, subthalamus[FMA]. The more posterior and ventral of two forebrain neuromeres, the other being the telencephalon; major derivatives are the eye cups, the brain pretectal region, the thalamus, hypothalamus, and epithalamus (including the habenula and epiphysis).  * *Comment* = Note that in ABA, this is part of the brain stem
