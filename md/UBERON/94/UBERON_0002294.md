
## Class : biliary system

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002294
 * *Label* = biliary system
 * *Definition* = Organ system subdivision that consists of the organs and ducts that are involved in the production and transportation of bile. In most species this is the gallbladder and the bile ducts (biliary tree). [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biliary_system ]
