
## Class : jugular vein

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004711
 * *Label* = jugular vein
 * *Definition* = Any one of the several large veins of the neck that drain blood from the head[BTO]. Veins in the neck which drain the brain, face, and neck into the brachiocephalic or subclavian veins[GAID]. The jugular veins are veins that bring deoxygenated blood from the head back to the heart via the superior vena cava[WP].
