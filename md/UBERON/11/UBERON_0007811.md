
## Class : craniocervical region

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007811
 * *Label* = craniocervical region
 * *Definition* = The anteriormost subdivision of the body that includes the head, jaws, pharyngeal region and the neck (if present).
