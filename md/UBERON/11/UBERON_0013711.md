
## Class : posterior inferior iliac spine

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013711
 * *Label* = posterior inferior iliac spine
 * *Definition* = The posterior border of the ala, shorter than the anterior, also presents two projections separated by a notch, the posterior superior iliac spine and the posterior inferior iliac spine. The posterior inferior iliac spine corresponds with the posterior extremity of the auricular surface.
