
## Class : rib 12

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004611
 * *Label* = rib 12
 * *Definition* = In humans: The twelfth rib has a single articular facet on the head, which is of rather large size. It has no necks or tubercles, and is pointed at its anterior ends. The twelfth has angle nor costal groove; it is much shorter than the eleventh rib, and its head is inclined slightly downward. Sometimes the twelfth rib is even shorter than the first rib.
