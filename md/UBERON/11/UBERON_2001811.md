
## Class : infraorbital sensory canal

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001811
 * *Label* = infraorbital sensory canal
 * *Definition* = Sensory canal lateral or ventral to and in close proximity to the otic and supraorbital sensory canals at their union. The infraorbital sensory canal is bilaterally paired and associated with the infraorbital bones. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:wd]
