
## Class : ventral posterolateral nucleus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002942
 * *Label* = ventral posterolateral nucleus
 * *Definition* = The ventral posterolateral nucleus (VPL) is a nucleus of the thalamus. Together with the ventral posteromedial nucleus (VPM) it constitutes the ventral posterior nucleus.
