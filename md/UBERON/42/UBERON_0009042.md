
## Class : prostatic venous plexus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009042
 * *Label* = prostatic venous plexus
 * *Definition* = The prostatic veins form a well-marked prostatic plexus which lies partly in the fascial sheath of the prostate and partly between the sheath and the prostatic capsule.
