
## Class : sciatic nerve

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001322
 * *Label* = sciatic nerve
 * *Definition* = A large nerve that supplies nearly the whole of the skin of the leg, the muscles of the back of the thigh, and those of the leg and foot.
