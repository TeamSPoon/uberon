
## Class : pancreatic bud

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003922
 * *Label* = pancreatic bud
 * *Definition* = The embryonic pancreas develops from two separate anlagen in the foregut epithelium, one dorsal and two ventral pancreatic buds[PMID]. In humans, an embryonic structure that is an outgrowth of the duodenum during embryogenesis - joins together to form the adult pancreas[WP].
