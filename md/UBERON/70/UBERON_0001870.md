
## Class : frontal cortex

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001870
 * *Label* = frontal cortex
 * *Definition* = Anterior portion of the frontal cortex, lying anterior to the central sulcus in humans. It is bounded by the parietal cortex posteriorly and the temporal cortex laterally. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = NIF_GrossAnatomy:nlx_anat_20090601 ]
 * *Comment* = Editor note: Many species don't have lobes but they do have frontal cortex. Lobe isn't a really well defined term though [MM]
