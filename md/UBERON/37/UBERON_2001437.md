
## Class : ductus communicans

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001437
 * *Label* = ductus communicans
 * *Definition* = The constriction between the anterior and posterior chambers of the swim bladder. There does not appear to be an identifiable sphincter in the ductus, and therefore other musculature may regulate its opening. Finney et al, 2006.
