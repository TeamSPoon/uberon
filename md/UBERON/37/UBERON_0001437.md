
## Class : epiphysis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001437
 * *Label* = epiphysis
 * *Definition* = Subdivision of long bone which forms its expanded proximal or distal articular end; together with other the subdivisions of long bone, it constitutes the long bone.
