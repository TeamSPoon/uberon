
## Class : epiphysis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001437
 * *Label* = epiphysis
 * *Definition* = The head of a long bone that articulates with a neighboring skeletal element and is separated from the shaft by the epiphyseal plate until bone growth stops. At that time, the plate disappears and the head and shaft are united[MESH,modified].
