
## Class : tertiary ovarian follicle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000037
 * *Label* = tertiary ovarian follicle
 * *Definition* = Ovarian follicle is the basic unit of female reproductive biology and is composed of roughly spherical aggregations of cells found in the ovary. They contain a single oocyte (aka ovum or egg). These structures are periodically initiated to grow and develop, culminating in ovulation of usually a single competent oocyte. These eggs/ova are only developed once every menstrual cycle (i. e, once a month). [WP,unvetted]. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Graafian_follicle]
