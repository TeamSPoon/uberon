
## Class : intercalarium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000525
 * *Label* = intercalarium
 * *Definition* = Neural arch and Weberian ossicle that has been modified in shape relative to other neural arches. Intercalarium includes three parts: an anterolateral process (the manubrium) which is embedded in the interossicular ligament and the ascending and articulating processes.
