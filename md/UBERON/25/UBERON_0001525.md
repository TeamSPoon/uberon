
## Class : extensor carpi radialis brevis muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001525
 * *Label* = extensor carpi radialis brevis muscle
 * *Definition* = The Extensor carpi radialis brevis is shorter and thicker than the extensor carpi radialis longus. The longus muscle can be found above the distal end of the Extensor carpi radialis brevis. [WP,unvetted].
