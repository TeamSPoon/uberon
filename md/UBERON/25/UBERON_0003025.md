
## Class : brachium of inferior colliculus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003025
 * *Label* = brachium of inferior colliculus
 * *Definition* = The Brachium of inferior colliculus (or inferior brachium) carries auditory afferent fibers from the inferior colliculus of the mesencephalon to the medial geniculate nucleus.
