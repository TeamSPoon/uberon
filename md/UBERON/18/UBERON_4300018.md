
## Class : ventral marginal cartilage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4300018
 * *Label* = ventral marginal cartilage
 * *Definition* = In the clasper skeleton, a flat semicylindrical cartilage that is partially fused to the lateral edge of the axial cartilage, and forms the lateral wall of the clasper groove. [Compagno, L. 2001. Sharks of the World: An Annotated and Illustrated Catalogue of Shark Species Known to Date. Volume 2]
