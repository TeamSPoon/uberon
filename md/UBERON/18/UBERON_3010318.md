
## Class : obsolete iridophore

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3010318
 * *Label* = obsolete iridophore
 * *Definition* = A pigment cell derived from the neural crest. The cell contains flat light-reflecting platelets, probably of guanine, in stacks called reflecting platets or iridisomes.
