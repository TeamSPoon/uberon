
## Class : forelimb bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008962
 * *Label* = forelimb bone
 * *Definition* = A bone that is part of a forelimb region. Examples: humerus, any of the phalanges. Counter-example: scapula (a bone of the pectoral girdle). Note that we consider the forelimb to end at the shoulder. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = https://orcid.org/0000-0002-6601-2165 ]
