
## Class : cortical intermediate zone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004040
 * *Label* = cortical intermediate zone
 * *Definition* = the region of the developing mammalian cortex that extends between the ventricular zone and the cortical plate (CP); normally, the IZ is a region of tangential migration of cells, and at midgestation, the lower part of the IZ develops into the subventricular zone [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0008457 ]
