
## Class : cuneocerebellar tract

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002640
 * *Label* = cuneocerebellar tract
 * *Definition* = White matter tract that originates from the cuneate and external cuneate nuclei.  It relays ascending spinal fibers in the dorsal funiculus and conveys information from forelimbs to the cerebellum.
