
## Class : yolk sac

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001040
 * *Label* = yolk sac
 * *Definition* = Membranous sac attached to an embryo, providing early nourishment in the form of yolk in bony fishes, sharks, reptiles, birds, and primitive mammals. It functions as the developmental circulatory system of the human embryo, before internal circulation begins. In the mouse, the yolk sac is the first site of blood formation, generating primitive macrophages and erythrocytes. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Yolk_sac ]
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: In therians, a structure homologous to the yolk sac is is present, but contains no yolk platelets. Instead it is filled with fluid [ISBN10:0073040584 "Vertebrates, Kardong"]
