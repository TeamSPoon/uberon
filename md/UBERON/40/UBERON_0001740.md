
## Class : arytenoid cartilage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001740
 * *Label* = arytenoid cartilage
 * *Definition* = The arytenoid cartilages are a pair of small three-sided pyramids which form part of the larynx, to which the vocal cords are attached. Each is pyramidal in form, and has three surfaces, a base, and an apex. [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Arytenoid_cartilage ]
