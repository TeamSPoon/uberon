
## Class : posterior lateral line nerve

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000175
 * *Label* = posterior lateral line nerve
 * *Definition* = Cranial nerve which enters the brain between cranial nerves VIII and IX; contains afferents and sensory efferents to the posterior lateral line ganglion and middle ganglion. Fibers from the posterior lateral line ganglion innervate the occipital dorsal lateral line and trunk lateral lines. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = ZFIN:curator ]
