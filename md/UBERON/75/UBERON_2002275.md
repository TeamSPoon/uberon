
## Class : Jakubowski's organ

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002275
 * *Label* = Jakubowski's organ
 * *Definition* = Anatomical cluster that includes the terminal supraorbital neuromast(s) contained in the nasal bone and innervated by the buccal nerve, contact between the nasal bone and lacrimal, and contact between the supraorbital and infraorbital canals.
