
## Class : sacral neural crest

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010075
 * *Label* = sacral neural crest
 * *Definition* = One of the 5 distinct and partially overlapping functional domains of the premigratory neural crest. The vagal and sacral neural crest cells develop into the ganglia of the enteric nervous system, also known as the parasympathetic ganglia.
