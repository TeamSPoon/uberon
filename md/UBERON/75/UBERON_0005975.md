
## Class : hair follicle bulge

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005975
 * *Label* = hair follicle bulge
 * *Definition* = the area located in the bottom of the permanent portion of a resting (telogen) hair follicle where the cells of the external root sheath of the hair follicle are contiguous with the basal layer of the epidermis; this portion of the follicle is involved in the generation of a new hair at the onset of the hair growth cycle (anagen) and contains the hair follicle stem cells [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0009759 ]
