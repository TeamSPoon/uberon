
## Class : pila preoptica

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3010565
 * *Label* = pila preoptica
 * *Definition* = A strut of cartilage  extending dorsally from the basal plate and with other similar cartilages forming the sides of the brain case. Posterior to the pila preoptica is the pila antoptica between these two cartilages emerges the optic and trochlear nerves.
