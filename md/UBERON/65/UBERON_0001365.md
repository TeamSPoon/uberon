
## Class : sacro-iliac joint

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001365
 * *Label* = sacro-iliac joint
 * *Definition* = the joint in the bony pelvis between the sacrum and the ilium of the pelvis, which are joined by strong ligaments. In humans, the sacrum supports the spine and is supported in turn by an ilium on each side. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Sacroiliac_joint]
