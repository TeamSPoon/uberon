
## Class : exocrine gland

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002365
 * *Label* = exocrine gland
 * *Definition* = A gland that secretes products (excluding hormones and other chemical messengers) into ducts (duct glands) which lead directly into the external environment[WP]. Typical exocrine glands include sweat glands, salivary glands, mammary glands, stomach, liver, pancreas [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Exocrine_gland ]
