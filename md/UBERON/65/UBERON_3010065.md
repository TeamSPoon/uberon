
## Class : M. gracilis major

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3010065
 * *Label* = M. gracilis major
 * *Definition* = Originates by a broad tendon from the postero-ventral surface of the pelvic rim and inserts by two tendons. One broad tendon inserts on the knee aponeurosis, and in part on the ventro-medial surface of the tibiofibula. The other long tendon inserts on the posterior surface of the tibiofibula just below the proximal head. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + AAO:EJS]
