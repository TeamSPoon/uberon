
## Class : accessory vomerine tooth plate

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001930
 * *Label* = accessory vomerine tooth plate
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone that is located lateral to vomer off midline of palate. Accessory vomerine tooth plate bears teeth and may be present as one or more pairs. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:wd]
 * *Comment* = Accessory vomerine tooth plate is not synonymous with dermopalatine tooth plate, palatine tooth plate, or pterygoid tooth plate.
