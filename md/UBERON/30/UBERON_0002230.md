
## Class : head of rib

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002230
 * *Label* = head of rib
 * *Definition* = The head of the rib is the end of a rib closest to the vertebral column, with which it articulates with. It is marked by a kidney-shaped articular surface, divided by a horizontal crest into two facets for articulation with the depression formed on the bodies of two adjacent thoracic vertebrae; the upper facet is the smaller; to the crest is attached the interarticular ligament.
