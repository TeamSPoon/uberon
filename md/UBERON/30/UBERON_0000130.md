
## Class : transverse foramen

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000130
 * *Label* = transverse foramen
 * *Definition* = Foramen that pierces the transverse processes of the seven cervical vertebrae. In the upper six vertebrae, the foramen gives passage to the vertebral artery, vertebral vein, and a plexus of sympathetic nerves. The seventh foramen lacks the artery, but contains the vein and sympathetic nerves. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transverse_foramen ]
