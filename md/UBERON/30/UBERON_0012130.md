
## Class : olecranon fossa

 * *Definition* = Above the back part of the trochlea of the humerus is a deep triangular depression, the olecranon fossa, in which the summit of the olecranon is received in extension of the forearm. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Olecranon_fossa]
