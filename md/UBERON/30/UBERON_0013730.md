
## Class : mycetome

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013730
 * *Label* = mycetome
 * *Definition* = A specialized organ that is linked to the gut in beetles and host to a symbiotic yeast. The yeast cells assist in the digestion of less nutritious foods, supply needed B-vitamins and sterols, and provide resistance to certain toxins.
