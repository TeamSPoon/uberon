
## Class : inferior prenasal cartilage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000234
 * *Label* = inferior prenasal cartilage
 * *Definition* = Elongate cartilage that extends anteroventrally from the solum nasi to abut the ventral portion of the posterior aspect of the alary process of the premaxilla. It was first described by Wiedersheim in 1876. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = AAO:LAP ]
