
## Class : dorsal plus ventral thalamus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001897
 * *Label* = dorsal plus ventral thalamus
 * *Definition* = A midline paired symmetrical structure within the brains of vertebrates, including humans. It is situated between the cerebral cortex and midbrain, both in terms of location and neurological connections. Its function includes relaying sensation, special sense and motor signals to the cerebral cortex, along with the regulation of consciousness, sleep and alertness. The thalamus surrounds the third ventricle. It is the main product of the embryonic diencephalon. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Thalamus ]
