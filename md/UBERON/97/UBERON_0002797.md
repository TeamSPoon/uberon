
## Class : dorsal trigeminal tract

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002797
 * *Label* = dorsal trigeminal tract
 * *Definition* = The dorsal trigeminal tract (dorsal trigeminothalamic tract, or lemniscus) is a tract which receives signals from Meissner's corpuscles and Pacinian corpuscles. this tract arises from Principal trigeminal nucleus and terminates in the VPM nucleus of the thalamus. [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Dorsal_trigeminal_tract ]
 * *Comment* = This tract arises from Principal trigeminal nucleus and terminates in the ipsilateral VPM nucleus of the thalamus.[WP]
