
## Class : stratum argenteum of choroid

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013397
 * *Label* = stratum argenteum of choroid
 * *Definition* = A silver reflective layer in the outer choroid, adjacent to the sclera, frequently present in fish larvae but not as frequent in adults. In species known to have a stratum argenteum as adults, like the toadfish (Toad- fishes), the tapetum lucidum is absent or vestigial and there is little choroidal melanin.
