
## Class : anterior spinal artery

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005431
 * *Label* = anterior spinal artery
 * *Definition* = In human anatomy, the anterior spinal artery is the blood vessel that supplies the anterior portion of the spinal cord. It arises from branches of the vertebral arteries and is supplied by the anterior segmental medullary arteries, including the artery of Adamkiewicz, and courses along the anterior aspect of the spinal cord.
