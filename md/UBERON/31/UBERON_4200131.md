
## Class : trochanteric shelf

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200131
 * *Label* = trochanteric shelf
 * *Definition* = Anatomical projection on the anterolateral region of the proximal end of the femur.
 * *Comment* = In Archosaurs was originally a possible insertion site for the m. iliofemoralis externus (Hutchinson 2001). In more derived dinosaurs this becomes a distinct process possibly providing the attachment surface for mm. iliotrochanteri in addition to m. iliofemoralis externus (Carrano and Hutchinson 2002)
