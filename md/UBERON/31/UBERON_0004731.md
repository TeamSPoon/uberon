
## Class : neuromere

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004731
 * *Label* = neuromere
 * *Definition* = A transverse unitary subdivision of the neural tube that shares a common dorsoventral structure (floor, basal, alar, and roof plates), but each having differential molecular identities and fates; they comprise the secondary prosencephalon, diencephalon (prosomeres), the midbrain (mesomeres), and the hindbrain (rhombomeres). [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + DOI:10.1016/j.tins.2013.06.004]
 * *Comment* = We take the definition of neuromere from Puelles et al, although the existence of mesomeres and prosomeres may not be widely accepted
