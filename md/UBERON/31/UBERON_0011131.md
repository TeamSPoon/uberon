
## Class : intermetacarpal joint

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011131
 * *Label* = intermetacarpal joint
 * *Definition* = Synovial joint between the bases of adjacent metacarpals. Examples: intermetacarpal joint of right second and third metacarpal bones, intermetacarpal joint of right fourth and fifth metacarpal bones.
