
## Class : gastric pit

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000424
 * *Label* = gastric pit
 * *Definition* = Gastric pits are indentations in the stomach which denote entrances to the gastric glands. They are deeper in the pylorus than they are in the other parts of the stomach.
