
## Class : irregular connective tissue

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011821
 * *Label* = irregular connective tissue
 * *Definition* = Connective tissue, which consists of a population of connective tissue cells, the intercellular matrix of which contains an irregular network of collagen and elastic fiber bundles. Examples: areolar tissue, mucoid tissue, connective tissue of peritoneum, connective tissue of fibrous pericardium. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = FMA:20107 ]
