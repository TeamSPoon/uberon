
## Class : vomeronasal nerve

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009121
 * *Label* = vomeronasal nerve
 * *Definition* = Nerve carrying fibers from the vomeronasal organ to the accessory olfactory bulb[AAO]. originates in the olfactory epithelial cells of Jacobson's organ, and that passes through the submucous tissue of the nasal septum and the cribriform plate of the ethmoid bone to the olfactory bulb[BTO].
