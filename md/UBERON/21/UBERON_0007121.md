
## Class : skin nerve field

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007121
 * *Label* = skin nerve field
 * *Definition* = A zone of skin that is mainly innervated by a single sensory nerve. A nerve field innervated by exactly one dorsal root is called a dermatome. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = UBERON:cjm ]
