
## Class : ora serrata of retina

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011221
 * *Label* = ora serrata of retina
 * *Definition* = The ora serrata is the serrated junction between the retina and the ciliary body. This junction marks the transition from the simple non-photosensitive area of the retina to the complex, multi-layered photosensitive region. In animals in which the region does not have a serrated appearance, it is called the ora ciliaris retinae. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Ora_serrata ]
