
## Class : myelencephalon

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005290
 * *Label* = myelencephalon
 * *Definition* = The posterior part of the developing vertebrate hindbrain or the corresponding part of the adult brain composed of the medulla oblongata and a portion of the fourth ventricle; as well as the glossopharyngeal nerve (CN IX), vagus nerve (CN X), accessory nerve (CN XI), hypoglossal nerve (CN XII), and a portion of the vestibulocochlear nerve (CN VIII).[BTO,WP]. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + BTO:0000758[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Myelencephalon]
 * *Comment* = the terms metencephalon and myelencephalon are only meaningful in mammals and birds - Neuroanatomy of the Zebrafish Brain. Note that its not clear if this refers to the developing medulla oblongata - MA (adult) has two distinct classes
