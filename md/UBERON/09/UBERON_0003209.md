
## Class : blood nerve barrier

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003209
 * *Label* = blood nerve barrier
 * *Definition* = barrier between the perineurium of peripheral nerves and the vascular endothelium of endoneurial capillaries. The perineurium acts as a diffusion barrier, but ion permeability at the blood-nerve barrier is still higher than at the blood-brain barrier[GO].
