
## Class : palpebral bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010309
 * *Label* = palpebral bone
 * *Definition* = An ossified element investing the upper eyelid. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + PMID:16496288[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Palpebral_(bone)]
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: The most extreme development of the palpebral is found in the armored dino- saur Euoplocephalus tutus, where the bone forms a quarter-sphere and adducts to completely occlude the or- bit (Coombs, 1972; Vickaryous and Russell, 2003). Notwithstanding the superficially similar position, palpe- brals and tarsal plates differ both anatomically and developmentally and are not considered homologous (Gauthier et al., 1988).
