
## Class : cusp of cardiac valve

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006009
 * *Label* = cusp of cardiac valve
 * *Definition* = Organ component which has as its parts the cusp proper, lunule and nodule of semilunar valvule[FMA]. The cusps of the heart valves serve to seal the heart valves when closed. There are three cusps for each valve except for the mitral valve, which has only two (hence its alternate name, 'bicuspid valve'). 'Nodules' are located at the tip of the valve, to form a tighter seal[WP].
