
## Class : fascia of Camper

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007809
 * *Label* = fascia of Camper
 * *Definition* = The superficial layer (fascia of Camper) is a thick superficial layer of the anterior abdominal wall. It is areolar in texture, and contains in its meshes a varying quantity of adipose tissue. It is found superficial to Scarpa's fascia. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fascia_of_Camper ]
