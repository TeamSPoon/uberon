
## Class : coronal suture

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002489
 * *Label* = coronal suture
 * *Definition* = The coronal suture is a dense, fibrous connective tissue joint that separates the frontal and parietal bones of the skull. At birth, the bones of the skull do not meet. [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Coronal_suture ]
