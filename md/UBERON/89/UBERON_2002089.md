
## Class : gular plate

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002089
 * *Label* = gular plate
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone located on the ventral surface of the skull and positioned between the two rami of the lower jaw. Anteriorly the gular attaches to the symphysis of the lower jaw through fibrous connective tissue. The gular is an unpaired median bone.
