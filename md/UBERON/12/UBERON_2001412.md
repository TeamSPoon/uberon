
## Class : epiotic

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001412
 * *Label* = epiotic
 * *Definition* = Endochondral bone that borders that medial edge of the posttemporal fossa. The epiotics are sutured to parietals and supraoccipital dorsally, the exoccipitals ventrally and the pterotics laterally. The epiotic is a paired bone.
