
## Class : Brodmann (1909) area 27

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013558
 * *Label* = Brodmann (1909) area 27
 * *Definition* = Area 27 of Brodmann-1909 is a cytoarchitecturally defined cortical area that is a rostral part of the parahippocampal gyrus of the guenon (Brodmann-1909). It is commonly regarded as a synonym of presubiculum (Crosby-62). [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brodmann_area_27 ]
