
## Class : iliolumbar vein

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001320
 * *Label* = iliolumbar vein
 * *Definition* = The iliolumbar vein is the vena comitans of the iliolumbar artery. The obturator nerve crosses superficial to it. A single vein is found more commonly than a double vein. It drains vertebral segments four and five. It is closely related to the ascending lumbar vein. [WP,unvetted].
