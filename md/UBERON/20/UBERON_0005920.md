
## Class : superior nasal concha

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005920
 * *Label* = superior nasal concha
 * *Definition* = The back part of the medial surface of the labyrinth of ethmoid is subdivided by a narrow oblique fissure, the superior meatus of the nose, bounded above by a thin, curved plate, the superior nasal concha. The bone found in superior nasal concha is part of the ethmoid bone. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Superior_nasal_concha ]
