
## Class : pronator teres

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001520
 * *Label* = pronator teres
 * *Definition* = The pronator teres is a muscle of the human body (located mainly in the forearm) that, along with the pronator quadratus, serves to pronate the forearm (turning it so the palm faces posteriorly).
