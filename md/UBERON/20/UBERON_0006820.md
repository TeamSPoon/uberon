
## Class : body of sternum

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006820
 * *Label* = body of sternum
 * *Definition* = The middle and longest portion of the sternum, lying between the manubrium superiorly and the xiphoid process inferiorly; it considerably lengthier, narrower, and thinner than the manubrium, attains its greatest breadth close to the lower end.  * *Comment* = AAO - Proximal portion of the sternum, usually elaborated into a stylum that may be ossified. [AAO:0000939]
