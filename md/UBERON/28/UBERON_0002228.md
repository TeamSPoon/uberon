
## Class : rib

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002228
 * *Label* = rib
 * *Definition* = An intersegmental rod-shaped bone that forms in the peritoneal membrane and attach to the vertebral parapophyses. They protect and support internal organs. In most vertebrates, ribs surround the chest. they enable lungs to expand by expanding the chest, they also protect the lungs, heart, and other internal organs of the thorax. In some animals, especially snakes, ribs may provide support and protection for the entire body.
