
## Class : presphenoid bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008828
 * *Label* = presphenoid bone
 * *Definition* = The anterior part of the body of the sphenoid bone in front of the basisphenoid. It is usually a separate bone in the young or fetus, but becomes a part of the sphenoid in the adult.
