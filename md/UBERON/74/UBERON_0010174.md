
## Class : Schweigger-Seidel sheath

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010174
 * *Label* = Schweigger-Seidel sheath
 * *Definition* = a phagocytic sleeve that is part of a sheathed arteriole of the spleen, and is sometimes referred to as a splenic ellipsoid.
