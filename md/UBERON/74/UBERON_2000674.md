
## Class : interopercle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000674
 * *Label* = interopercle
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone that is part of the opercular series. It is located ventro-medially to the preopercle and anterior to the subopercle. It is joined to the posterior part of the lower jaw (retroarticular) by a ligament. The interopercle is paired. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:wd]
