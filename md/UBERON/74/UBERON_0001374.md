
## Class : biceps femoris

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001374
 * *Label* = biceps femoris
 * *Definition* = The biceps femoris is a muscle of the posterior (the back) thigh. As its name implies, it has two parts, one of which (the long head) forms part of the hamstrings muscle group.
