
## Class : male genital duct

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006947
 * *Label* = male genital duct
 * *Definition* = A duct or series of ducts that transports sperm from the gonad. In mammals this is from the seminiferous tubules through rete testis, vas efferentia, epididymis, vas deferens, ejeculatory duct to the urethra.
