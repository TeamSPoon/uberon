
## Class : intercostal lymph node

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0012236
 * *Label* = intercostal lymph node
 * *Definition* = The intercostal lymph nodes (intercostal glands ) occupy the posterior parts of the intercostal spaces, in relation to the intercostal vessels. They receive the deep lymphatics from the postero-lateral aspect of the chest; some of these vessels are interrupted by small lateral intercostal glands.
