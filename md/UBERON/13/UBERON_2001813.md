
## Class : preopercular sensory canal

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001813

 * *Definition* = Sensory canal lateral or ventral to and in close proximity with the otic and post-otic sensory canals at their union, and anteroventrally with the mandibular sensory canal.
