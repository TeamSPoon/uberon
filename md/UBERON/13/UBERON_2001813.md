
## Class : preopercular sensory canal

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001813
 * *Label* = preopercular sensory canal
 * *Definition* = Sensory canal lateral or ventral to and in close proximity with the otic and post-otic sensory canals at their union, and anteroventrally with the mandibular sensory canal. The preopercular sensory canal is bilaterally paired and associated with the preopercle bone. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:wd]
 * *Comment* = May be continuous with the otic and post-otic sensory canals at their union.
