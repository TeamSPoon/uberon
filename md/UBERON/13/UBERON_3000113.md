
## Class : crista intermedia

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000113
 * *Label* = crista intermedia
 * *Definition* = Paired, oblique plates of cartilage located ventral to the alary cartilages. The dorsomedial margin of each crista intermedia is fused to the transitional zone between the septum nasi and the tectum nasi, whereas the ventrolateral margin bears two laminae, the lamina superior and the lamina inferior.
