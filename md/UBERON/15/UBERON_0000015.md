
## Class : anatomical boundary

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000015
 * *Label* = anatomical boundary
 * *Definition* = Structural anatomical relation which holds between each anatomical entity of one to three dimensions and some immaterial anatomical entity of one lower dimension such that the latter demarcates (delimits) the former from its neighborhood.
