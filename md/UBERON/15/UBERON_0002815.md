
## Class : horizontal fissure of cerebellum

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002815
 * *Label* = horizontal fissure of cerebellum
 * *Definition* = The largest and deepest fissure in the cerebellum is named the horizontal sulcus (or horizontal fissure). It commences in front of the pons, and passes horizontally around the free margin of the hemisphere to the middle line behind, and divides the cerebellum into an upper and a lower portion.
