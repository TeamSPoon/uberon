
## Class : stifle joint

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011123
 * *Label* = stifle joint
 * *Definition* = A complex joint in the hind limbs of quadruped mammals such as the sheep, horse or dog. It is the equivalent joint to the human knee.
