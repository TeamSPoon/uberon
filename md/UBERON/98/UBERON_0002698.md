
## Class : preoccipital notch

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002698
 * *Label* = preoccipital notch
 * *Definition* = Small indentation on the inferior surface of the cerebral cortex at the border of the occiptal and parietal lobes. It is considered as a landmark because the occipital lobe is located just behind the line that connects that notch with the parietoccipital sulcus (adapted from Wikipedia).
