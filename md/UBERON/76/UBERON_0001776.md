
## Class : optic choroid

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001776
 * *Label* = optic choroid
 * *Definition* = vascular layer containing connective tissue, of the eye lying between the retina and the sclera. The choroid provides oxygen and nourishment to the outer layers of the retina. Along with the ciliary body and iris, the choroid forms the uveal tract[WP]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Choroid ]
