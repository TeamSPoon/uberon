
## Class : temporal pole

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002576
 * *Label* = temporal pole
 * *Definition* = Anterior component of the temporal lobe (rostral boundary) extends caudally to the entorhinal cortex.  The medial and lateral boundaries are the medial aspect of the temporal lobe and the superior or inferior temporal sulci, respectively (Christine Fennema-Notestine).
