
## Class : ventral nuclear group

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002776

 * *Definition* = Nuclei in the ventral part of the thalamus, involved in modulating activity of dorsal thalamic nuclei. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + ISBN10:0471888893]
