
## Class : infraorbital

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000376
 * *Label* = infraorbital
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone that is located anterior, ventral or posterior to the eye. Infraorbital bones may or may not contain the infraorbital sensory canal. Infraorbital bones are numbered starting with the anteriormost element of the series. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = ZFIN:curator ]
