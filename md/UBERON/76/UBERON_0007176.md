
## Class : superior angle of scapula

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007176
 * *Label* = superior angle of scapula
 * *Definition* = An angle of the scapula formed by the junction of the superior and vertebral borders. It is thin, smooth, rounded, inclined somewhat lateralward, and gives attachment to a few fibers of the Levator scapulae[WP,unvetted] [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Superior_angle_of_scapula]
