
## Class : scala media

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002295
 * *Label* = scala media
 * *Definition* = the division of the spiral canal of the cochlea that contains the organ of Corti (the neuroepithelial receptor organ for hearing) [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0003169,MGI:anna,PMID:1680563 ]
 * *Comment* = located in between the scala tympani and the scala vestibuli, separated by the basilar membrane and Reissner's membrane (the vestibular membrane) respectively. Scala media houses the organ of Corti. [Wikipedia:Scala_media]
