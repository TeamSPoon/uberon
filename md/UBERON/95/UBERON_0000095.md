
## Class : cardiac neural crest

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000095
 * *Label* = cardiac neural crest
 * *Definition* = portion of neural crest that develops from the dorsal neural tube. It overlaps the vagal neural crest and migrates to populate the pharyngeal arches 3, 4 and 6 (producing structures in the head) and to the heart, forming connective tissue that separates the great vessels of the heart.
