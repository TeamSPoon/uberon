
## Class : orbital sulcus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002595
 * *Label* = orbital sulcus
 * *Definition* = The inferior or orbital surface of the frontal lobe is concave, and rests on the orbital plate of the frontal bone. It is divided into four orbital gyri by a well-marked H-shaped orbital sulcus [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Orbital_sulcus ]
