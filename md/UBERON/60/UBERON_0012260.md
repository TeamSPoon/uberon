
## Class : alular digit

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0012260
 * *Label* = alular digit
 * *Definition* = A manual digit found in Aves that is the most preaxial position and is morphologically similar to digit 1 in the standard tetrapod configuration and develops from embryonic digit 2, with contributions from embryonic digit 1. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = PMID:10220427, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = PhenoscapeRCN:Oct2012 ]
 * *Comment* = Hypothesized to be homologous to manual digit 1 of other tetrapods. Develops from the embryonic digit that is positionally the 2nd, with contributions from the 1st
