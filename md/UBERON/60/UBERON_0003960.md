
## Class : styloid process of temporal bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003960
 * *Label* = styloid process of temporal bone
 * *Definition* = slender needle-like pointed projection that runs downward and slightly forward from the base of the inferior surface of the petrous portion of the temporal bone where it joins the tympanic portion; it gives attachment to the styloglossus, stylohyoid, and stylopharyngeus muscles and the stylohyoid and stylomandibular ligaments [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + MP:0008023[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Temporal_styloid_process]
 * *Comment* = Disease notes: Eagle's syndrome
