
## Class : spine appendage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008260
 * *Label* = spine appendage
 * *Definition* = a hard, thorny or needle-like structure which occurs on various animals. Animals such as porcupines and sea urchins grow spines as a self-defense mechanism. Spines are often formed of keratin. Other animals with spines are hedgehogs, spiny mice and at least two ornithischian dinosaurs.
