
## Class : pectoral flipper tubercle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013527
 * *Label* = pectoral flipper tubercle
 * *Definition* = A bump or swelling adorining the head and leading edge of a pectoral flipper, associated with the multiple joints and terminal phalanges as found in the multiple phalanges of the humpback whale
 * *Comment* = In a humpback the number of tubercles ranges from 9 to 11, with the first at the joint between the radius and metacarpal of digit II (with digit I absent)
