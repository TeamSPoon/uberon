
## Class : minor calyx

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001227
 * *Label* = minor calyx
 * *Definition* = The minor calyx, in the kidney, surrounds the apex of the malpighian pyramids. Urine formed in the kidney passes through a papilla at the apex into the minor calyx then into the major calyx. Peristalsis of the smooth muscle originating in pace-maker cells originating in the walls of the calyces propels urine through the pelvis and ureters to the bladder. [WP,unvetted].
