
## Class : orbital part of frontal bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011627
 * *Label* = orbital part of frontal bone
 * *Definition* = A zone of the frontal bone that consists of two thin triangular plates, the orbital plates, which form the vaults of the orbits, and are separated from one another by a median gap, the ethmoidal notch.
