
## Class : orbital part of frontal bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011627
 * *Label* = orbital part of frontal bone
 * *Definition* = The orbital or horizontal part of the frontal bone (pars orbitalis) consists of two thin triangular plates, the orbital plates, which form the vaults of the orbits, and are separated from one another by a median gap, the ethmoidal notch.
