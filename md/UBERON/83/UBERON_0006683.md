
## Class : posterior fontanelle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006683
 * *Label* = posterior fontanelle
 * *Definition* = In humans, the posterior fontanelle (or occipital fontanelle) is a gap between bones in the skull, triangular in form and situated at the junction of the sagittal suture and lambdoidal suture. It generally closes in 6Ð8 weeks from birth. A delay in closure is associated with congential hypothyroidism.
