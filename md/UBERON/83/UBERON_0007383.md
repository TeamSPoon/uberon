
## Class : enveloping layer of ectoderm

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007383
 * *Label* = enveloping layer of ectoderm
 * *Definition* = Outermost monolayer of cells surrounding the embryo that become very flattened in the blastula and give rise to the periderm. Originally this tissue is one cell layer thick but in most vertebrates it soon becomes a two layered structure. The outer layer gives rise to the periderm.
