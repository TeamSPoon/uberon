
## Class : medullary ray

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009883
 * *Label* = medullary ray
 * *Definition* = A renal, cortical lobule, consisting of the ascending or descending limbs of the loop of Henle or of the collecting tubules; medullary rays are regions where parallel arrays of straight tubules travel perpendicular to the capsule and extend from the cortex to the medulla. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + MP:0011347[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Bone_marrow_of_ovaryry_ray_(anatomy)]
 * *Comment* = Termonology notes: Their name is potentially misleading -- the "medullary" refers to their destination, not their location. They are located only in the renal cortex, and not in the renal medulla. AO notes: FMA says parenchyma, MP says cortex
