
## Class : carpometacarpus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011205
 * *Label* = carpometacarpus
 * *Definition* = The carpometacarpus is the fusion of the carpal and metacarpal bone, essentially a single fused bone between the wrist and the knuckles. It is a smallish bone in most birds, generally flattened and with a large hole in the middle. In flightless birds, however, its shape may be slightly different, or it might be absent entirely.
