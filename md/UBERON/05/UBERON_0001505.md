
## Class : coracobrachialis muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001505
 * *Label* = coracobrachialis muscle
 * *Definition* = The Coracobrachialis is the smallest of the three muscles that attach to the coracoid process of the scapula. (The other two muscles are pectoralis minor and biceps brachii. ) It is situated at the upper and medial part of the arm. It is perforated by and innervated by the musculocutaneous nerve.
