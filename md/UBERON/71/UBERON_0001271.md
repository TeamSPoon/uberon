
## Class : pelvic girdle region

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001271
 * *Label* = pelvic girdle region
 * *Definition* = Subdivision of pelvic complex which links the free portion of the paired limb/fin to the trunk[FMA,modified]. Examples: There are only two instances, right and left pelvic girdle regions.
