
## Class : gluteus medius

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001371
 * *Label* = gluteus medius
 * *Definition* = One of three gluteal muscles, is a broad, thick, radiating muscle, situated on the outer surface of the pelvis. Its posterior third is covered by the gluteus maximus, its anterior two-thirds by the gluteal aponeurosis, which separates it from the superficial fascia and integument. [WP,unvetted].
