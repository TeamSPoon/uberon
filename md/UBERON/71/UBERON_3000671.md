
## Class : anterolateral process of hyoid plate

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000671
 * *Label* = anterolateral process of hyoid plate
 * *Definition* = Processes that flank the hyales laterally and extend anterolaterally from the anterolateral margin of the hyoid plate. They are formed as a result of new cartilaginous growth lateral to the basibranchial. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = AAO:LAP ]
