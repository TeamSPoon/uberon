
## Class : temporal lobe

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001871
 * *Label* = temporal lobe
 * *Definition* = Temporal lobe is the ventrolateral lobe of five lobes comprising each cerebral hemisphere. It is bounded dorsally by the lateral fissure and posteriorly by an arbitrary border shared with the occipital lobe.
