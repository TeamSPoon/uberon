 * *Definition* = Skeletal subdivision of dermal bones that may include the anterolateral plate (AL), the spinal process (Sp), and anteroventral plate (AV) and other dermal bones that are part of the pectoral girdle. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = VSAO:curator ]
