
## Class : aortic sac

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005432
 * *Label* = aortic sac
 * *Definition* = The dilated structure that is lined by endothelial cells and located at the arterial pole of the heart just above (distal to) the truncus arteriosus in mammalian embryos; it is the primordial vascular channel from which the aortic arches (and eventually the dorsal aortae) arise; the aortic sac is homologous to the ventral aorta of gill-bearing vertebrates [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0012510 ]
