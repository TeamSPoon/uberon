
## Class : pharyngeal tonsil

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001732
 * *Label* = pharyngeal tonsil
 * *Definition* = Adenoids (or pharyngeal tonsils, or nasopharyngeal tonsils) are a mass of lymphoid tissue situated at the very back of the nose, in the roof of the nasopharynx, where the nose blends into the mouth. Normally, in children, they make a soft mound in the roof and posterior wall of the nasopharynx, just above and behind the uvula. [WP,unvetted].
