
## Class : heterocercal tail

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200054
 * *Label* = heterocercal tail
 * *Definition* = Type of tail (caudal) fin in which the vertebral column turns upwards into the upper lobe which is longer than the lower. Asymmetrical externally as well as internally.
 * *Comment* = Elasmobranchii, Acipenseridae, Polyodontidae and in many larval Teleostomi. May also be used for an asymmetrical caudal fin but with epicercal defining upper lobe longer and hypocercal the lower lobe longer. http://www.briancoad.com/dictionary/DicPics/heterorercal.htm
