
## Class : Malpighian tubule

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001054
 * *Label* = Malpighian tubule
 * *Definition* = A tubule that extends from the posterior part of the digestive tract which absorbs solutes, water, and wastes from the surrounding hemolymph. Each tubule consists of a single layer of cells that is closed off at the distal end with the proximal end joining the alimentary canal at the junction between the midgut and hindgut[WP,modified].
