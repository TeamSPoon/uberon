
## Class : obsolete lepidotrichium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001554
 * *Label* = obsolete lepidotrichium
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone that is rod-like, formed and usually contained in a fin membrane, and jointed to or adjacent to distal radials. Lepidotrichia are formed from paired hemitrichia surrounding actinotrichia. There are usually two more lepidotrichia than distal radials.
