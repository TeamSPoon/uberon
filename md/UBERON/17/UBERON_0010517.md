
## Class : cephalic clasper

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010517
 * *Label* = cephalic clasper
 * *Definition* = A mace-like spiny-headed rod found on the mid-dorsal surface of heads of male Holocephali. Thought to aid in holding the female during copulation.
