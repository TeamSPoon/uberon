
## Class : obsolete motor nerve

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001026
 * *Label* = obsolete motor nerve
 * *Definition* = General anatomical term applied to nerves in which somatic efferent nerve fibers predominate[FMA]. A motor nerve is an efferent nerve that exclusively contains the axons of somatic and branchial motoneurons, which innervate skeletal muscles (that ensure locomotion) and branchial muscles (that motorize the face and neck)[WP].
