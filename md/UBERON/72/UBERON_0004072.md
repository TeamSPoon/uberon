
## Class : cerebellum globose nucleus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004072
 * *Label* = cerebellum globose nucleus
 * *Definition* = the two or three small masses of gray matter that is located medial to the emboliform nucleus and lateral to the fastigial nucleus; it receives axons from the intermediate area of the cerebellar hemispheres and its afferents exit through the superior cerebellar peduncle; it is recognized in human cerebellum but is not distinguishable in all mammalian species [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0009982 ]
