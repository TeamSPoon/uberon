
## Class : posterolateral process

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000672
 * *Label* = posterolateral process
 * *Definition* = Paired processes that extend posterolaterally from the posterolateral margin of the hyoid plate.
