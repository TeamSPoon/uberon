
## Class : immature gonad

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2005272
 * *Label* = immature gonad
 * *Definition* = Immature reproductive tissue that has not undergone final maturation into either testis or ovary. During gonadal development, the gonad may be transiently intersexual where there is a parallel presence of both oocytes and spermatocytes.
