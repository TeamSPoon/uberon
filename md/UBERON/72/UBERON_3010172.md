
## Class : subarticular tubercles

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3010172
 * *Label* = subarticular tubercles
 * *Definition* = Is a integumentary structure located on the ventral surface of the hand and/or foot. Found at the junction between, in the hand, the metacarpal and most proximal phalanx of the digit and between phalanges, and in the foot between the metatarsal and most proximal phalanx of the digit and between phalanges.
