
## Class : opercular part of inferior frontal gyrus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002980
 * *Label* = opercular part of inferior frontal gyrus
 * *Definition* = The part of the inferior frontal gyrus that lies between the inferior precentral sulcus and the ascending ramus of the lateral sulcus. It is called opercularis because it covers part of the insula. The pars opercularis together with the pars triangularis form Broca's area.
