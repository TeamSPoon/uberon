
## Class : median dorsal plate

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200102
 * *Label* = median dorsal plate
 * *Definition* = Dermal armour plate that is situated behind the head and constitute the dorsal most portion of the dermal shoulder girdle. Modified from Brazeau 2009, Young 2010
 * *Comment* = Present in some basal gnathostomes.
