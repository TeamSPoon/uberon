
## Class : anterior distal serration of pectoral fin spine

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002002
 * *Label* = anterior distal serration of pectoral fin spine
 * *Definition* = Process that is retrorse, thorn-like and arising from the anteriorly-facing margin of the abducted pectoral spine. Anterior distal serration of pectoral fin spine is usually present on the distal section of the pectoral fin spine. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:wd ]
