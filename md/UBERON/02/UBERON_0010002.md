
## Class : pulmonary neuroendocrine body

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010002
 * *Label* = pulmonary neuroendocrine body
 * *Definition* = corpuscular, organoid structures composed of PNECs, found as distinctive innervated clusters only within intrapulmonary airways, where they appear concentrated at airway branch points; NEBs reach from the basement membrane to the airway lumen and are thought to function as oxygen sensors.
