
## Class : M. flexor antibrachii lateralis superficialis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3010743
 * *Label* = M. flexor antibrachii lateralis superficialis
 * *Definition* = Originates as a superior and inferior head. The superior head originates on the outer edge of the humerus and the inferior head originates on the lateral epicondyle of the humerus.
