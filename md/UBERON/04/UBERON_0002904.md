
## Class : lateral occipital sulcus

 * *Label* = lateral occipital sulcus
 * *Definition* = In the occipital lobe, the lateral occipital sulcus extends from behind forward, and divides the lateral surface of the occipital lobe into a superior and an inferior gyrus, which are continuous in front with the parietal and temporal lobes.
