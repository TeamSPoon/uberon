
## Class : lamina orbitonasalis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006604
 * *Label* = lamina orbitonasalis
 * *Definition* = Cranial cartilage which is bilaterally paired and develops as a dorsal plate-like projection of the anterolateral ethmoid plate, which are oriented perpendicular to the ethmoid plate. It fuses dorsally to the taenia marginalis anterior, and it joins the ethmoid cartilage medially. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = ZFA:0001503 ]
