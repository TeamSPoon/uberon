
## Class : median nuchal line

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0014804
 * *Label* = median nuchal line
 * *Definition* = Of the nuchal lines, the one that runs from the external occipital protuberance a ridge or crest is the median nuchal line, often faintly marked, descends to the foramen magnum, and affords attachment to the ligamentum nuchae.
