
## Class : inner chondrogenic layer of perichondrium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008304
 * *Label* = inner chondrogenic layer of perichondrium
 * *Definition* = The perichondrium consists of two separate layers: an outer fibrous layer and inner chondrogenic layer.
