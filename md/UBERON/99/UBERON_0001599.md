
## Class : stapedius muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001599
 * *Label* = stapedius muscle
 * *Definition* = The muscle that stabilizes the stapes bone. The stapedius emerges from a pinpoint foramen in the apex of the pyramidal eminence (a hollow, cone-shaped prominence in the posterior wall of the tympanic cavity), and inserts into the neck of the stapes. [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stapedius_muscle ]
