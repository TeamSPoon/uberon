
## Class : stapedius muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001599
 * *Label* = stapedius muscle
 * *Definition* = The stapedius is the smallest skeletal muscle in the human body. At just over one millimeter in length, its purpose is to stabilize the smallest bone in the body, the stapes. The stapedius emerges from a pinpoint foramen in the apex of the pyramidal eminence (a hollow, cone-shaped prominence in the posterior wall of the tympanic cavity), and inserts into the neck of the stapes.
