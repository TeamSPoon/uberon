
## Class : hippocampal sulcus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002899
 * *Label* = hippocampal sulcus
 * *Definition* = The hippocampal sulcus, also known as the hippocampal fissure, is a sulcus that separates the dentate gyrus from the subiculum and the CA1 field in the hippocampus. [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Hippocampal_sulcus ]
 * *Comment* = AO notes - two classes in ncit (in general ncit distinguishes between fissure and sulcus, whereas FMA treats these as exact synonyms)
