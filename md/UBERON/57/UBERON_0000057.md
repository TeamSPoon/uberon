
## Class : urethra

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000057
 * *Label* = urethra
 * *Definition* = A tube which connects the urinary bladder to the outside of the body. In males, the urethra travels through the penis, and carries semen as well as urine. In females, the urethra is shorter and emerges above the vaginal opening. The external urethral sphincter is a striated muscle that allows voluntary control over urination.
