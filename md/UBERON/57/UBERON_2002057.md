
## Class : gill opening

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002057
 * *Label* = gill opening
 * *Definition* = Anatomical space that is the external opening of a gill slit connecting the pharyngeal cavity to the exterior. Gill opening is usually paired and located on the lateral or ventral surfaces of the head. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:wd]
 * *Comment* = In teleosts, there is typically a single gill opening posterior to the operculum.
