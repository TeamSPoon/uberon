
## Class : laryngotracheal groove

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006257
 * *Label* = laryngotracheal groove
 * *Definition* = a groove in the floor of the pharynx formed from the ventral diverticulum of endoderm that arises from the floor of the foregut caudal to the pharynx. This structure is the precursor for the larynx and trachea[WP,edited].
