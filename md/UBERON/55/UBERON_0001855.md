
## Class : cochlear duct of membranous labyrinth

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001855
 * *Label* = cochlear duct of membranous labyrinth
 * *Definition* = An endolymph filled cavity inside the cochlea, located in between the scala tympani and the scala vestibuli, separated by the basilar membrane and Reissner's membrane (the vestibular membrane) respectively. Houses the organ of Corti[WP]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cochlear_duct ]
