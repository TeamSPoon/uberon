
## Class : caudate nucleus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001873
 * *Label* = caudate nucleus
 * *Definition* = Subcortical nucleus of telecephalic origin consisting of an elongated gray mass lying lateral to and bordering the lateral ventricle. It is divided into a head, body and tail in some species. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = NIF_GrossAnatomy:birnlex_1373 ]
