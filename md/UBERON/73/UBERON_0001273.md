
## Class : ilium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001273
 * *Label* = ilium
 * *Definition* = Paired endochondral bone that is the dorsal-most of the pelvic bones, offering attachment areas for gluteal muscles on its main surface [PHENOSCAPE:ad]. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + PHENOSCAPE:ad]
 * *Comment* = Note that this is_a hip bone in MA. Taxon notes: All reptiles have an ilium except snakes, although some snake species have a tiny bone which is considered to be an ilium[WP]
