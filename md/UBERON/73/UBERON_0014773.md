
## Class : medial palpebral artery

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0014773
 * *Label* = medial palpebral artery
 * *Definition* = The medial palpebral arteries (internal palpebral arteries) are arteries of the head. They are two in number, superior and inferior, arise from the ophthalmic, opposite the pulley of the Obliquus superior.
