
## Class : fossa maxillaris

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000652
 * *Label* = fossa maxillaris
 * *Definition* = Depression lacated on the inner surface of the maxilla between the palatine process and the pars palatina. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + AAO:LAP]
 * *Comment* = Alternative more fish orientated definition.

 A fossa that is a depression in the proximal end of the maxilla involved in articulation with the palatine. [Baldwin, CC and Johnson GD. 1996. Interrelationships of Aulopiformes. Pages 355-404 in MLJ Stiassny, LR Parenti, and DG Johnson, (eds.), Interrelationships of fishes. Academic Press, San Diego, CA.]
