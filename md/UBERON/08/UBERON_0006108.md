
## Class : corticomedial nuclear complex

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006108
 * *Label* = corticomedial nuclear complex
 * *Definition* = These structures are referred to as nuclei but have a layered cortical like structure. These areas include the anterior amygdaloid area, posterior amygdaloid area, the periamygdaloid cortex, and the bed nucleus of the accessory olfactory tract.
