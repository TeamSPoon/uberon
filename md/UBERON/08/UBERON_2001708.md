
## Class : dermosphenotic

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001708
 * *Label* = dermosphenotic
 * *Definition* = Infraorbital that is the last bone of the infraorbital series, carrying the infraorbital canal, and sometimes also the supraorbital, temporal, and/or preopercular canals. The dermosphenotic is sometimes sutured or fused completely to the skull, e.g. to the autosphenotic. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:wd]
