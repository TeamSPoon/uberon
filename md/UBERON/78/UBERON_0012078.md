
## Class : fovea capitis of femur

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0012078
 * *Label* = fovea capitis of femur
 * *Definition* = The surface of the head of the femur is smooth, coated with cartilage in the fresh state, except over an ovoid depression, the fovea of head of femur, which is situated a little below and behind the center of the head, and gives attachment to the ligamentum teres. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Fovea_of_head_of_femur ]
 * *Comment* = Absent in pinnipeds
