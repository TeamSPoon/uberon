
## Class : epiphyseal plate

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002516
 * *Label* = epiphyseal plate
 * *Definition* = A hyaline cartilage plate in the metaphysis at each end of a long bone. The plate is found in children and adolescents; in adults, who have stopped growing, the plate is replaced by an epiphyseal line. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epiphyseal_plate ]
