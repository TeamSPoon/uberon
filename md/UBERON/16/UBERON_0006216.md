
## Class : cervical sinus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006216
 * *Label* = cervical sinus
 * *Definition* = a temporary depression caudal to the second pharyngeal arch, containing the succeeding pharyngeal arches; it is overgrown by the second pharyngeal arch and closes off as the cervical vesicle[TFD]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Cervical_sinus, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://medical-dictionary.thefreedictionary.com/cervical+sinus ]
 * *Comment* = This sinus is bounded in front by the hyoid arch, and behind by the thoracic wall; it is ultimately obliterated by the fusion of its walls.
