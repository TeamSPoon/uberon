
## Class : cervical sinus of His

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006216
 * *Label* = cervical sinus of His
 * *Definition* = A temporary depression caudal to the second pharyngeal arch, containing the succeeding pharyngeal arches; it is overgrown by the second pharyngeal arch and closes off as the cervical vesicle. This sinus is bounded in front by the hyoid arch, and behind by the thoracic wall.
