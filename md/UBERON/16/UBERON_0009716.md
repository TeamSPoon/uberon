
## Class : cupular organ

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009716
 * *Label* = cupular organ
 * *Definition* = Epithelial sense organ containing the cupula housing a gelatinous material elongated from the basement of the organ toward the atrial cavity. The basement of the cupular organ consists of two cell types: sensory cells and support cell [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = DOI:10.2108/zsj.27.842 ]
 * *Comment* = Homology: possible homologues of neuromasts in the inner ear (Bone & Ryan, 1978; Wada et al 1998). In the tunicate cupular organ, the sensory cell is a primary neuron sending an axon to the CNS. The sensory cilium is within a deep indentation of the cell and is surrounded by a collar of short microvilli.
