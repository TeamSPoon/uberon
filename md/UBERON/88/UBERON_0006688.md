
## Class : sublingual caruncle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006688
 * *Label* = sublingual caruncle
 * *Definition* = The sublingual caruncle is a site on either side of the frenulum linguae on the sublingual surface of the tongue. There it denotes the site at which the submandibular gland empties into the oral cavity. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Sublingual_caruncle ]
