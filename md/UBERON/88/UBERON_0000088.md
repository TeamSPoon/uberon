
## Class : trophoblast

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000088
 * *Label* = trophoblast
 * *Definition* = Aggregate of cells forming the outer layer of a blastocyst, which provide nutrients to the embryo and develop into a large part of the placenta. They are formed during the first stage of pregnancy and are the first cells to differentiate from the fertilized egg. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Trophoblast ]
 * *Comment* = Development notes: The postimplantation derivatives of the trophectoderm, which make up most of the fetal part of the placenta[PMID:19829370]
