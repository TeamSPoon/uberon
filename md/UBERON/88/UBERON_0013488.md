
## Class : panniculus adiposus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013488
 * *Label* = panniculus adiposus
 * *Definition* = The panniculus adiposus is the fatty layer of the subcutaneous tissues, superficial to a deeper vestigial layer of muscle, the panniculus carnosus. An example is the Fascia of Camper. Another example is the superficial cervical fascia[WP] [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Panniculus_adiposus ]
 * *Comment* = It includes structures that are considered fascia by some sources but not by others. Consider merging with subcutaneous adipose tissue
