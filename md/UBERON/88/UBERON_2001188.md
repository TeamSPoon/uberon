
## Class : Weberian apparatus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001188
 * *Label* = Weberian apparatus
 * *Definition* = Anatomical cluster that consists of the anteriormost vertebrae and associated structures that connect the swim bladder to the inner ear. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + ZFIN:curator]
 * *Comment* = Vertebra 1-4, and sometimes vertebra 5 in some catfishes, are part of the Weberian apparatus. Weberian apparatus is present in Otophysi.
