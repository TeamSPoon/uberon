
## Class : stegural

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002167
 * *Label* = stegural
 * *Definition* = Modified neural arch, probably of ural centrum 4, formed by a bony base that may extend along the dorso-lateral surfaces of ural centra and preural centra 1 or 2 and by a well-developed antero-dorsal membranous projection that extends lateral to the base of epural(s) and anterior toward the neural arches and/or spines of preural centra 1 or 2 (Arratia and Schultze, 1992). The stegural is a paired bone. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + TAO:GA_TG]
