
## Class : embryonic urethral groove

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013241
 * *Label* = embryonic urethral groove
 * *Definition* = The urethral groove is a temporary linear indentation on the underside (ventral side) of the male penis during embryonic development. In humans, it typically appears around 8 weeks of gestation and becomes closed into a normal male urethra by the 12th week.
