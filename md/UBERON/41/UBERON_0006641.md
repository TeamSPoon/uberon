
## Class : appendix epididymis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006641
 * *Label* = appendix epididymis
 * *Definition* = On the head of the epididymis is a small stalked appendage (sometimes duplicated). It is named the appendix of the epididymis (or pedunculated hydatid), and is usually regarded as a detached efferent duct. This structure is derived from the Wolffian duct as opposed to the appendix testis which is derived from the mullerian duct remnant.
