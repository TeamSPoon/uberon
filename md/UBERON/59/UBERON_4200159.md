
## Class : ventral ridge system

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200159
 * *Label* = ventral ridge system
 * *Definition* = A collection of attached ridges on the ventrl surface of the femur consisting of the internal and fourth trochanters plus the adductor blade and crest. Most notible in early amniotes (based on Romer 1956)
