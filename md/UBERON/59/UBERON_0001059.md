
## Class : pars intercerebralis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001059
 * *Label* = pars intercerebralis
 * *Definition* = a neurosecretory center of the insect brain, located along the anterior midline[wiktionary]. A medial cleft or depression dividing the left and right protocerebrum and containing numerous large and small somata of neurosecretory and neuromodulatory neurons[FBbt].
