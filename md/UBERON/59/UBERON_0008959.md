
## Class : auditory bulla

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008959
 * *Label* = auditory bulla
 * *Definition* = A hollow bony structure on the ventral, posterior portion of the skull of placental mammals that encloses parts of the middle and inner ear. In most species, it is formed by the tympanic part of the temporal bone. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Auditory_bulla ]
