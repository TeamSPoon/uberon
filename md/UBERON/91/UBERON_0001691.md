
## Class : external ear

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001691
 * *Label* = external ear
 * *Definition* = Part of the ear external to the tympanum (eardrum). It typically consists of a tube (the external auditory meatus) that directs sound waves on to the tympanum, and may also include the external pinna, which extends beyond the skull[GO].
