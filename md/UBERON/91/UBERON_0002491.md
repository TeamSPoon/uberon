
## Class : lambdoid suture

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002491
 * *Label* = lambdoid suture
 * *Definition* = The lambdoid suture (or lambdoidal suture) is a dense, fibrous connective tissue joint on the posterior aspect of the skull that connects the parietal and temporal bones with the occipital bone. Its name comes from its lambda-like shape. [WP,unvetted]. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Lambdoid_suture]
