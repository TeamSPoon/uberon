
## Class : multilaminar epithelium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000486
 * *Label* = multilaminar epithelium
 * *Definition* = Epithelium which consists of more than one layer of epithelial cells that may or may not be in contact with a basement membrane.
