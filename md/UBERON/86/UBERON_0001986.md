
## Class : endothelium

 * *Label* = endothelium
 * *Definition* = A layer of epithelium that lines the heart, blood vessels (endothelium, vascular), lymph vessels (endothelium, lymphatic), and the serous cavities of the body[MESH]. Simple squamous epithelium which lines blood and lymphatic vessels and the heart[FMA] [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = FMA:63916, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MESH:A10.272.491 ]
