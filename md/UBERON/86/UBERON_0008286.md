
## Class : feather calamus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008286
 * *Label* = feather calamus
 * *Definition* = The hollow basal portion of a feather below the vane[BTO]. At the base of the feather, the rachis expands to form the hollow tubular calamus (or quill) which inserts into a follicle in the skin. The basal part of the calamus is without vanes. This part is embedded within the skin follicle and has an opening at the base (proximal umbilicus) and a small opening on the side (distal umbilicus)[WP].
