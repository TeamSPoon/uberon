
## Class : horizontal septum

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003901
 * *Label* = horizontal septum
 * *Definition* = A logitudinal sheet of continuous connective tissue partition developing at the apex of the chevron-shaped myotome and separating dorsal (epaxial) and ventral (hypaxial) body wall muscle masses; each is destined to become, respectively, the epaxial and hyopaxial musculature.
