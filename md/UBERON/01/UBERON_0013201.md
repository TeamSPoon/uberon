
## Class : olfactory pathway

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013201
 * *Label* = olfactory pathway
 * *Definition* = Set of nerve fibers conducting impulses from olfactory receptors to the cerebral cortex. It includes the olfactory nerve; olfactory bulb; olfactory tract, olfactory tubercle, anterior perforated substance, and olfactory cortex. The term rhinencephalon is restricted to structures in the CNS receiving fibers from the olfactory bulb. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + MESH:A08.186.211.577.699]
