
## Class : chitin-based cuticle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001001
 * *Label* = chitin-based cuticle
 * *Definition* = A cuticular covering that is composed primarily of chitin. The main structural component of arthropod cuticle is a polysaccharide, chitin, composed of N-acetylglucosamine units, together with proteins and lipids[WP].
