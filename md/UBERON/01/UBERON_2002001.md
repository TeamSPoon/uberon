
## Class : anterior dentation of pectoral fin spine

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002001
 * *Label* = anterior dentation of pectoral fin spine
 * *Definition* = Process that is tooth- or thorn-like and arising from the anteriorly-facing margin of the abducted pectoral spine. Anterior dentation of pectoral fin spine is usually present on the proximal and/or middle section of the pectoral fin spine. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:wd ]
