
## Class : levator labii superioris alaeque nasi

 * *Label* = levator labii superioris alaeque nasi
 * *Definition* = The levator labii superioris alaeque nasi muscle is, translated from Latin, the 'lifter of the upper lip and of the wing of the nose'.
