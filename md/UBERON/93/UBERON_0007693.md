
## Class : caroticotympanic artery

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007693
 * *Label* = caroticotympanic artery
 * *Definition* = The caroticotympanic branch (tympanic branch) is small; it enters the tympanic cavity through a minute foramen in the carotid canal, and anastomoses with the anterior tympanic branch of the internal maxillary, and with the stylomastoid artery.
