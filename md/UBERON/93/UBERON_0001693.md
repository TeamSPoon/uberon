
## Class : exoccipital bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001693
 * *Label* = exoccipital bone
 * *Definition* = Paired cartilage bone that bears a large foramen (lateral occipital foramen). Contacts the supraoccipital dorsally, the epiotic laterally and the basioccipital ventrally. Forms the posterior hind margin of the cranium and borders the lateral and posterior margins of the forum (foramen) magnum.
