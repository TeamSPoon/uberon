 * *Label* = protractor operculi
 * *Definition* = Muscle located in the lateral region of the cheek, linking the lateral surface of opercle with the posterolateral margin of the suspensorium (usually the preopercle). [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:wd ]
