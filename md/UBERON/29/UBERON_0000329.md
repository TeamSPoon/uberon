
## Class : hair root

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000329
 * *Label* = hair root
 * *Definition* = The enlarged basal part of a hair within the skin[BTO]. The root of the hair ends in an enlargement, the hair bulb, which is whiter in color and softer in texture than the shaft, and is lodged in a follicular involution of the epidermis called the hair follicle.
