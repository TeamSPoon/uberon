
## Class : otic sensory canal

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001629
 * *Label* = otic sensory canal
 * *Definition* = Sensory canal between and in close proximity posteriorly with the post-otic and preoperculo-mandibular sensory canals, and anteriorly with the supraorbital and infraorbital sensory canals.
