
## Class : supraoptic nucleus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001929
 * *Label* = supraoptic nucleus
 * *Definition* = The supraoptic nucleus (SON) is a nucleus of magnocellular neurosecretory cells in the hypothalamus of the mammalian brain. The nucleus is situated at the base of the brain, adjacent to the optic chiasm. [WP,unvetted].
