
## Class : parietal pleura

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002400
 * *Label* = parietal pleura
 * *Definition* = the part of the pleura external to the visceral pleura. It lines the inner surface of the chest wall, covers the diaphragm, and is reflected over the structures occupying the middle of the thorax.
