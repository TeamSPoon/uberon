
## Class : corpuscles of Stannius

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001200
 * *Label* = corpuscles of Stannius
 * *Definition* = Islands of eosinic cells found on the lateroventral surface of the kidney. Function is thought to be that of the parathyroid gland in other vertebrates, which are lacking in fishes.
