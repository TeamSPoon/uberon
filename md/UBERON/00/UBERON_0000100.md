
## Class : blastopore

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000100
 * *Label* = blastopore
 * *Definition* = Structure around the opening into the archenteron during the embryonic stages of an organism. The distinction between protostomes and deuterostomes is based on the direction in which the mouth (stoma) develops in relation to the blastopore. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Blastopore ]
 * *Comment* = In protostome development, the first opening in development, the blastopore, becomes the animal's mouth; In deuterostome development, the blastopore becomes the animal's anus
