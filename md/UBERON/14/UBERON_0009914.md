
## Class : renal lobule

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009914
 * *Label* = renal lobule
 * *Definition* = the portion of a renal lobe consisting of nephrons grouped around a single medullary ray and draining into a single collecting duct; human kidneys have multilobular, multipapillary architecture while mice and rats have unilobular, unipapillary kidneys [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0011382, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Cortical_lobule ]
 * *Comment* = Alternate definition: One of the subdivisions of the kidney, consisting of a medullary ray and that portion of the convoluted port (renal corpuscles and convoluted tubules) associated with its collecting duct.  -http://www.mondofacto.com/facts/dictionary?lobulus+corticalis+renalis
