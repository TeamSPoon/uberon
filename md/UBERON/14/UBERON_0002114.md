
## Class : duodenum

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002114
 * *Label* = duodenum
 * *Definition* = The first part of the small intestine. At the junction of the stomach and the duodenum the alimentary canal is inflected. The duodenum first goes anteriorly for a short distance, turns dorsally, and eventually caudally, thus it is a U-shaped structure with two horizontal sections (a ventral and a dorsal one).
