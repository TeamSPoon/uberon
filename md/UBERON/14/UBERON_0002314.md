
## Class : midbrain tectum

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002314
 * *Label* = midbrain tectum
 * *Definition* = Dorsal part of the midbrain, consisting of the superior and inferior colliculi and the pretectal nuclei (MM). [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + NIF_GrossAnatomy:birnlex_1032]
 * *Comment* = In adult humans it is present only in the mesencephalon as the inferior and the superior colliculi[WP].
