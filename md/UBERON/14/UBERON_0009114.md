
## Class : cervical thymus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009114
 * *Label* = cervical thymus
 * *Definition* = A mature thymus that is entirely part of the cervical region. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = https://orcid.org/0000-0002-6601-2165 ]
 * *Comment* = A cervical thymus is a pathological structure in a human - it can form during the migration of the thymus to its location in the mediastinum. Some mice have a cervical thymus. There are examples of cervical thymi in marsupials and prosimians[ISBN-10:0781714125]
