
## Class : metanephric ascending thin limb

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005114
 * *Label* = metanephric ascending thin limb
 * *Definition* = The metanephric ascending thin limb is a segment of a nephron tubule in the metanephros lying in the inner medulla that is permeable to ions but not to water and has a simple epithelium; active transepithelial solute transport is absent[GO].
