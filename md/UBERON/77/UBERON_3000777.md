
## Class : epicoracoid horn

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000777
 * *Label* = epicoracoid horn
 * *Definition* = A pair of cartilaginous expansions (one from each epicoracoid cartilage) that project posteriorly. They articulate with the sternum by means of grooves, pouches or fossae in the dorsal surface of the sternum and provide a site for the insertion of a pair of muscles derived from the m. rectus abdominis.
