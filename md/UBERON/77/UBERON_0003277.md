
## Class : skeleton of upper jaw

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003277
 * *Label* = skeleton of upper jaw
 * *Definition* = A subdivision of the skeleton that corresponds to the upper part of the mouth. The lower jaw skeleton includes the following elements, when present: upper jaw teeth, the maxilla and other lower jaw bones.
