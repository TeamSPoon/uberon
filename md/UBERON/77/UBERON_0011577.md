
## Class : flexural organ

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011577
 * *Label* = flexural organ
 * *Definition* = a portion of tissue that is the most anterior portion of the notochord. The flexural organ secretes Reissner's fiber-related proteins, including F-spondin. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + ZFA:0007071[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + https://sourceforge.net/tracker/?group_id=76834&atid=994726]
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: may be an ontogenetical remnant of the infundibular organ in cephalochordates. Editor notes: we cannot classify as a secretory circumventricular organ, as this leads to unsatisfiability
