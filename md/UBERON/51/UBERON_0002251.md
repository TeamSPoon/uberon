
## Class : iliocostalis muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002251
 * *Label* = iliocostalis muscle
 * *Definition* = The iliocostalis is the muscle immediately lateral to the longissimus that is the nearest to the furrow that separates the epaxial muscles from the hypaxial. It lies very deep to the fleshy portion of the serratus ventralis (serratus anterior). [WP,unvetted]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Iliocostalis ]
