
## Class : ectoplacental cavity

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008851
 * *Label* = ectoplacental cavity
 * *Definition* = closed space within the ectoplacental cone, formed by the fusion of the parts of the amniotic fold that separate it from the amniotic cavity[MP]. a developmental cavity that exists in some mammals and is derived by division of the proamniotic space; it is further removed from the embryo than the amniotic cavity in some mammals [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0011207, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://www.medilexicon.com/medicaldictionary.php?s=epamniotic+cavity ]
