
## Class : skeleton of pes

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001445
 * *Label* = skeleton of pes
 * *Definition* = Subdivision of skeleton that is the collection of all skeletal elements in a pes, which is the distal section of the posterior hindlimb skeleton consisting of the mesopodium, the metapodium and the acropodium (e.g. including the ankle, sole, and toes)[Phenoscape].
