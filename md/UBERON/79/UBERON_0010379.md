
## Class : superior tarsal muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010379
 * *Label* = superior tarsal muscle
 * *Definition* = The superior tarsal muscle is a smooth muscle adjoining the levator palpebrae superioris muscle that helps to raise the upper eyelid. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Superior_tarsal_muscle]
 * *Comment* = Editors note: declaring this to be both smooth muscle and part of the LPS leads to a disjointness violation (smooth muscle and striated muscle)
