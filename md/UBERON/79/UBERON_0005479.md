
## Class : superior mesenteric ganglion

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005479
 * *Label* = superior mesenteric ganglion
 * *Definition* = In the upper part of the superior mesenteric plexus close to the origin of the superior mesenteric artery is a ganglion, the superior mesenteric ganglion. The superior mesenteric ganglion is the synapsing point for one of the pre- and post-synaptic nerves of the sympathetic division of the autonomous nervous system. This nerve goes on to innervate part of the large intestine. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Superior_mesenteric_ganglion ]
