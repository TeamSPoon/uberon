
## Class : hippocampus fimbria

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002310
 * *Label* = hippocampus fimbria
 * *Definition* = Regional part of fornix consisting of a prominent white matter structure adjacent to the hippocampus on the ventricular side continuous with the alveus. Near the splenium the fimbria separates from the hippocampus as the crus of the fornix. [adapted from Wikipedia] (MM: 2006-10-26).
