
## Class : rib 11

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004610
 * *Label* = rib 11
 * *Definition* = The eleventh rib has a single articular facet on the head, which is of rather large size. It has no necks or tubercles, and is pointed at its anterior ends.
