
## Class : masseteric fossa

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011310
 * *Label* = masseteric fossa
 * *Definition* = .The fossa for the masseter. In some therapsids, the coronoid process of the dentary bears a large external depression which accommodates the masseter -- an important muscle in closing and in grinding motions of the jaw.
