
## Class : gallbladder

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002110
 * *Label* = gallbladder
 * *Definition* = a small organ that aids digestion and stores bile produced by the liver[WP]. Organ with organ cavity which is continuous proximally with the cystic duct and distally terminates in the fundus of the gallbladder[FMA]. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + FMA:7202[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Gallbladder]
 * *Comment* = Rats do not have a gallbladder, but produce bile. The bile flows directly from the liver through the (hepatic) bile duct into the small intestine (Hebel and Stromberg, 1988)
