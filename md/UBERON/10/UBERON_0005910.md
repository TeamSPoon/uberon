
## Class : transitional epithelium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005910
 * *Label* = transitional epithelium
 * *Definition* = Multilaminar epithelium, which of consists of more than one layer of epithelial cells, in contact with a basement membrane, that can undergo distention and contraction without losing its integrity and consistency.
