
## Class : ectodermal placode

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005085
 * *Label* = ectodermal placode
 * *Definition* = An ectodermal placode is a thickening of the ectoderm that is the primordium of many structures derived from the ectoderm[GO] [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = GO:0060788 ]
 * *Comment* = Homology: With the exception of the adenohypophysis, homologues of the nonneurogenic placodes (e.g. placodes giving rise to the teeth, hair follicles and lens), appear to be lacking in invertebrate chordates.
