
## Class : greater omentum

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005448
 * *Label* = greater omentum
 * *Definition* = The greater omentum (also the great omentum, omentum majus, gastrocolic omentum, epiplošn, or, especially in animals, caul) is a large fold of peritoneum that hangs down from the stomach, and extends from the stomach to the posterior abdominal wall after associating with the transverse colon.
