
## Class : transverse pericardial sinus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002248
 * *Label* = transverse pericardial sinus
 * *Definition* = The passage between the venous and arterial mesocardia Ñi.e., between the aorta and pulmonary artery in front and the superior vena cava behind. Also, the sinus that forms in the pericardial cavity where the dorso-mesentary pericardium reside.[WP,unvetted].
