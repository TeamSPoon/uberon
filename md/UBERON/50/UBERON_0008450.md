
## Class : psoas muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0008450
 * *Label* = psoas muscle
 * *Definition* = A powerful flexor of the thigh at the hip joint (psoas major) and a weak flexor of the trunk and lumbar spinal column (psoas minor). Psoas is derived from the Greek "psoa", the plural meaning "muscles of the loin". It is a common site of infection manifesting as abscess (PSOAS ABSCESS). The psoas muscles and their fibers are also used frequently in experiments in muscle physiology.
