
## Class : infundibular recess of 3rd ventricle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006250
 * *Label* = infundibular recess of 3rd ventricle
 * *Definition* = The floor of the third ventricle is prolonged downward as a funnel-shaped recess, the infundibular recess, into the infundibulum, and to the apex of the latter the hypophysis or pititary is attached [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Infundibular_recess]
 * *Comment* = Editor note: in FMA this is a cavity, whereas in EHDAA2 it is an epithelial sac
