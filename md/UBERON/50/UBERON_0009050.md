
## Class : nucleus of solitary tract

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009050
 * *Label* = nucleus of solitary tract
 * *Definition* = A neural nucleus that is part of the solitary tract nuclear complex. The solitary tract and nucleus are structures in the brainstem that carry and receive visceral sensation and taste from the facial (VII), glossopharyngeal (IX) and vagus (X) cranial nerves.
