
## Class : olfactory placode

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003050
 * *Label* = olfactory placode
 * *Definition* = A thick plate of cells derived from the neural ectoderm in the head region of the embryo that develops into the olfactory region of the nasal cavity. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = XAO:0000005, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nasal_placode ]
 * *Comment* = Olfactory placodes are the only ectodermal placodes to produce glia, a cell type typically derived from neural crest // Note that NBK53171 classifies this as non-neurogenic
