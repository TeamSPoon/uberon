
## Class : gracilis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000950
 * *Label* = gracilis
 * *Definition* = The most superficial muscle on the medial side of the thigh. It is thin and flattened, broad above, narrow and tapering below. It arises by a thin aponeurosis from the anterior margins of the lower half of the symphysis pubis and the upper half of the pubic arch. [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + Wikipedia:Gracilis_muscle]
 * *Comment* = Editor's note: AAO has minor and major gracilis, which merge together, attaching the pelvic rim to the tibiofibula - this could be grouped with the mammalian gracilis at some level; note the current insertion site is 'tibia' which would be modified for amphibian tibiafibula
