
## Class : gracilis

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0000950
 * *Label* = gracilis
 * *Definition* = The most superficial muscle on the medial side of the thigh. It is thin and flattened, broad above, narrow and tapering below. It arises by a thin aponeurosis from the anterior margins of the lower half of the symphysis pubis and the upper half of the pubic arch.
