
## Class : opercle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000250
 * *Label* = opercle
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone that forms the postero-dorsal part of the opercular series. The opercle articulates with the hyomandibula antero-dorsally and with the subopercle ventrally. The opercle is a paired bone. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:GA_TG ]
 * *Comment* = The opercle is usually a large, flat bone that may be partially covered by the posterior region of the preopercle.
