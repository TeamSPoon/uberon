
## Class : tectospinal tract

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002949
 * *Label* = tectospinal tract
 * *Definition* = a long process of a CNS neuron, that carries efferent (outgoing) action potentials from the cell body in the superior colliculus of the midbrain towards target cells in the ventral spinal cord[GO]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Tectospinal_tract ]
