
## Class : tadpole stage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0009849
 * *Label* = tadpole stage
 * *Definition* = wholly aquatic larval stage in the life cycle of an amphibian, particularly of a frog or toad[WP]. XAO: The stages from feeding to the end of metamorphosis. Encompasses Weisz's first-form tadpole (equivalent to NF stages 45 to 49), second-form tadpole (NF 49 to 56) and third-form tadpole (NF 56 to 60-plus) stages.
