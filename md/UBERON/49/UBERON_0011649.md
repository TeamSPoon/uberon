
## Class : levator operculi

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011649
 * *Label* = levator operculi
 * *Definition* = Hyoid muscle that originates at the ventrolateral margin of the pterotic and inserts in the dorsomesial edge of the opercle.  The levator operculi is responible for jaw depression, its force of contraction is transmitted through the opercular series and the interoperculo-mandibular ligament to the lower jaw.
