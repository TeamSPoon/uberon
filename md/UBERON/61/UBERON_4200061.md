
## Class : epicercal tail

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200061
 * *Label* = epicercal tail
 * *Definition* = Tail fin in which the the caudal part of the notochord tapers posterodorsally. Based on Pradel et al. 2007
 * *Comment* = Osteostracans are the only jawless vertebrates that share with gnathostomes an epicercal tail.
