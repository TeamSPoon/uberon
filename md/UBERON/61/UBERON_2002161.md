
## Class : ural centrum 4

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2002161
 * *Label* = ural centrum 4
 * *Definition* = A vertebral centrum that is located immediately posterior to ural centrum 3 (polyural terminology) and may fuse to ural centrum 3 early in ontogeny. Ural centrum 4 articulates with hypural 4 ventrally. Ural centrum 4 is an unpaired median bone. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:GA_TG ]
 * *Comment* = Ural centrum 4 lacks a neural arch in teleosts.
