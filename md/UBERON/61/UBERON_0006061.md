
## Class : process of vertebra

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006061
 * *Label* = process of vertebra
 * *Definition* = A process projection from a part of the vertebra such as centrum or arches. Examples: transverse process, diapophyses, parapophyses, and zygapophyses (both the cranial zygapophyses and the caudal zygapophyses).
