
## Class : mylohyoid muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001564
 * *Label* = mylohyoid muscle
 * *Definition* = The mylohyoid muscle is a muscle running from the mandible to the hyoid bone, forming the floor of the oral cavity. These muscles are mesodermal in origin. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Mylohyoid_muscle ]
 * *Comment* = mylohyoideus and digastricus anterior of rats derived from intermandibularis posterior, see e.g. Jarvik [53] http://www.biomedcentral.com/1471-213X/8/24/table/T3
