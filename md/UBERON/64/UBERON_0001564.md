
## Class : mylohyoid muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0001564
 * *Label* = mylohyoid muscle
 * *Definition* = The mylohyoid is a pharyngeal arch I muscle that participates in oral/pharyngeal behaviors and is innervated by the nerve to mylohyoid and attaches to the midline raphe linking right and left mylohyoid and the mandible.
