
## Class : preopercle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000264
 * *Label* = preopercle
 * *Definition* = Dermal bone that is part of the opercular series and bears the preopercular sensory canal. The preopercle is a paired bone and typically L-shaped, with the horizontal limb overlying the interopercle and the vertical limb overlying the opercle.
