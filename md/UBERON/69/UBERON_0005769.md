
## Class : basement membrane of epithelium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005769
 * *Label* = basement membrane of epithelium
 * *Definition* = An acellular membrane that is part of the epithelium, lies adjacent to the epithelial cells, and is the fusion of the the basal lamina and the reticular lamina. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = UBERON:cjm, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Basement_membrane ]
 * *Comment* = This class represents a continuous sheet of basement membrane which can underlie multiple epithelial cells over large regions. In contrast, the GO class 'basal membrane' represents a portion of substance on the scale of a single cell.
