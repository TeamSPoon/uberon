
## Class : sternal keel

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200069
 * *Label* = sternal keel
 * *Definition* = The vertical plate of bone or cartilage attached to the median line of the sternum. Modified from Baumel et al. 1993
 * *Comment* = An enlarged ossified keel is found in most, but not all,  volant birds.
