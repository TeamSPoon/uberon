
## Class : superior cerebellar peduncle of midbrain

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007707
 * *Label* = superior cerebellar peduncle of midbrain
 * *Definition* = Regional part of superior cerebellar peduncle, located in the midbrain, consisting of a large bundle of fibers largely projecting from the deep cerebellar nuclei to the midbrain and thalamus.  It is continuous with the superior cerebellar peduncle of the pons. (MM). [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = NIF_GrossAnatomy:birnlex_1162 ]
