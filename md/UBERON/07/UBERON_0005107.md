
## Class : metanephric cap

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0005107
 * *Label* = metanephric cap
 * *Definition* = The metanephric cap is formed by the condensation of metanephric mesenchymal cells surrounding the ureteric bud tip[GO].
