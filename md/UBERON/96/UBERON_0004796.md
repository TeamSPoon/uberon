
## Class : prostate gland secretion

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0004796
 * *Label* = prostate gland secretion
 * *Definition* = A secretion of the prostate this is slightly alkaline fluid, milky or white in appearance. Usually constitutes 20Ð30% of the volume of the semen along with spermatozoa and seminal vesicle fluid [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Prostate#Secretions ]
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: Prostatic secretions vary among species. They are generally composed of simple sugars and are often slightly alkaline.
