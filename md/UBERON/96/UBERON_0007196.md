
## Class : tracheobronchial tree

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0007196
 * *Label* = tracheobronchial tree
 * *Definition* = the structure from the trachea, bronchi, and bronchioles that forms the airways that supply air to the lungs.
