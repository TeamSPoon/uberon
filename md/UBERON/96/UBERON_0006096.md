
## Class : posterior transverse temporal area 42

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006096
 * *Label* = posterior transverse temporal area 42
 * *Definition* = A subdivision of the cytoarchitecturally defined temporal region of cerebral cortex. It is located in the bank of the lateral sulcus on the dorsal surface of the temporal lobe. Cytoarchitecturally it is bounded medially by the anterior transverse temporal area 41(H) and laterally by the superior temporal area 22 (Brodmann-1909).
