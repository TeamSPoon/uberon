
## Class : medial superior olivary nucleus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0002782
 * *Label* = medial superior olivary nucleus
 * *Definition* = One of two subnuclei of the superior olive located within the caudal pontine tegmentum.  Appears as a parasagittal row of transversely oriented bipolar neurons in transverse sections of human, with elongated somata in humans and other primates and large caliber dendrites.
