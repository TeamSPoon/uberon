
## Class : interilial region

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_3000882
 * *Label* = interilial region
 * *Definition* = Area of convergence of the ilial shafts. When the preacetabular ilium is narrow, the interilial configuration is V-shaped or narrowly rounded, whereas when the interilial region is broadly expanded the interilial configuration is U-shaped.
