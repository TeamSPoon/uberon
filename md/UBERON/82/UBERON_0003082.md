
## Class : myotome

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0003082
 * *Label* = myotome
 * *Definition* = A group of tissues formed from somites that develop into the body wall muscle[WP]. Somitic compartment that is a precursor of muscle. mesoderm that is derived from the somite that is fated to become the musculature[MP]. Portion of the somites giving rise to body wall muscle masses[ZFA] [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = MP:0003939, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = PMID:17313522, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Myotome, [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = ZFIN:curator ]
 * *Comment* = Editors note: The term "myotome" is also used to describe the muscles served by a single nerve root / spinal segment - consider adding new class.
