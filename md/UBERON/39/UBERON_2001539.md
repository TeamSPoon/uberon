
## Class : basipterygium cartilage

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2001539
 * *Label* = basipterygium cartilage
 * *Definition* = Bilaterally paired small, rodlike cartilages on the ventral surface of the abdomen (present at 7.9 mm). Their posterior tips expand laterally, forming L-shaped cartilages. Medially, a posterior spur of cartilage develops, which will later ossify as the ischiac process (Weitzman 1962). [[database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) =  + ZFIN:curator]
