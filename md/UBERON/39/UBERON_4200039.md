
## Class : supraacetabular buttress

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_4200039
 * *Label* = supraacetabular buttress
 * *Definition* = A bony protuberance dorsal to the acetabulum on the ilium, confluent with the supraacetabular crest (when present). Acts as a bone brace to prevent excessive femoral abduction and the origin site for part of the M. iliofemoralis.
