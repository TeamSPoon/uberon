
## Class : mesocoracoid bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000239
 * *Label* = mesocoracoid bone
 * *Definition* = Endochondral bone that articulates laterally with the medial surface of the cleithrum and ventro-medially with the scapula and coracoid. Meoscoracoid bone is a small, arch-like bone that develops from the mesocoracoid cartilage. Mesocoracoid bone is paired. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:wd ]
