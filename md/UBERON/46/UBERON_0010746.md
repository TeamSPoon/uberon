
## Class : iliac blade

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010746
 * *Label* = iliac blade
 * *Definition* = The extension of the ilium above the level of the acetabulum. Attachment site for both hindlimb and caudal musculature. Medially is the site of attachment of the sacral ribs, where these exist[PHENOSCAPE:ad]. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = PHENOSCAPE:ad ]
 * *Comment* = Taxon notes: Often dorsally expanded in amniotes[ad].
