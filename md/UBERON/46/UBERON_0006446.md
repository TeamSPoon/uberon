
## Class : rostral middle frontal gyrus

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0006446
 * *Label* = rostral middle frontal gyrus
 * *Definition* = Component of the middle frontal gyrus The rostral boundary is the first appearance of the superior frontal sulcus whereas the caudal boundary is the middle frontal gyrus. The medial and lateral boundaries are the superior frontal sulcus and the inferior frontal sulcus respectively (Christine Fennema-Notestine).
