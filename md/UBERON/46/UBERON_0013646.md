
## Class : buccal nerve

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0013646
 * *Label* = buccal nerve
 * *Definition* = The buccal nerve (also called the long buccal nerve) is a nerve in the face. It is a branch of the mandibular nerve (which is itself a branch of the trigeminal nerve) and transmits sensory information from skin over the buccal membrane (in general, the cheek) and from the second and third molar teeth.
