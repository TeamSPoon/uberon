
## Class : entire pharyngeal arch associated mesenchyme

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010046
 * *Label* = entire pharyngeal arch associated mesenchyme
 * *Definition* = The sum total of mesenchymal tissue in the pharyngeal arch region. Pharyngeal mesenchyme is undifferentiated, loose connective tissue derived mostly from mesoderm, and also contains ectodermally derived neural crest cells.
