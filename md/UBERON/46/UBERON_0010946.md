
## Class : occipitofrontalis muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010946
 * *Label* = occipitofrontalis muscle
 * *Definition* = The occipitofrontalis or epicranius is a muscle which covers parts of the skull. It consists of two parts or bellies: The occipital belly, near the occipital bone, and the frontal belly, near the frontal bone. Some sources consider the occipitofrontalis to be a structure consisting of two distinct muscles, the Frontalis and the occipitalis. However, Terminologia Anatomica currently classifies it as a single muscle. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = Wikipedia:Occipitofrontalis_muscle ]
 * *Comment* = zone of muscle in FMA
