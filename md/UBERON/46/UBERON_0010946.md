
## Class : occipitofrontalis muscle

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0010946
 * *Label* = occipitofrontalis muscle
 * *Definition* = The occipitofrontalis or epicranius is a muscle which covers parts of the skull. It consists of two parts or bellies: The occipital belly, near the occipital bone, and the frontal belly, near the frontal bone.
