
## Class : parhypural

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_2000438
 * *Label* = parhypural
 * *Definition* = Haemal spine of last hemal arch that is pierced by the haemal canal through which the caudal vessels pass or haemal arch of preural centrum 1. Its distal part commonly supports lepidotrichia. The parhypural is an unpaired median haemal spine. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:GA_TG ]
