
## Class : mesethmoid bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011238
 * *Label* = mesethmoid bone
 * *Definition* = Endochondral bone that extends forward from the frontal bones and articulates posterolaterally with the lateral ethmoids and the vomer and parasphenoid ventrally. The mesethmoid is an unpaired median bone. [ [database_cross_reference](../../ef/oboInOwl#hasDbXref.md) = TAO:0000323 ]
 * *Comment* = absent in primitive mammals, ungulates, amphibians, reptiles/birds[Kardong]
