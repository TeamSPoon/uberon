
## Class : mesethmoid bone

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/UBERON_0011238
 * *Label* = mesethmoid bone
 * *Definition* = Endochondral bone that extends forward from the frontal bones and articulates posterolaterally with the lateral ethmoids and the vomer and parasphenoid ventrally.
