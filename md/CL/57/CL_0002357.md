
## Class : fetal derived definitive erythrocyte

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002357
 * *Label* = fetal derived definitive erythrocyte
 * *Definition* = A fetal liver derived enucleated erythrocyte. This erythrocyte resembles adult erythrocytes in that they are small (3- to 6- times smaller than primitive erythrocytes) and produce adult hemaglobins.
