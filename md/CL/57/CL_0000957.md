
## Class : large pre-B-II cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000957
 * *Label* = large pre-B-II cell
 * *Definition* = A large pre-B-II cell is a pre-B-II cell that is proliferating and is Rag1-negative and Rag2-negative.
