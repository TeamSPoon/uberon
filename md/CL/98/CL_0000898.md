
## Class : naive T cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000898
 * *Label* = naive T cell
 * *Definition* = Mature T cell not yet exposed to antigen with the phenotype CCR7-positive, CD45RA-positive, and CD127-positive. This cell type is also described as being CD25-negative, CD62L-high and CD44-low.
