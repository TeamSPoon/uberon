
## Class : T-helper 2 cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000546
 * *Label* = T-helper 2 cell
 * *Definition* = A CD4-positive, alpha-beta T cell that has the phenotype GATA-3-positive, CXCR3-negative, CCR6-negative, and is capable of producing interleukin-4.
