
## Class : clear cell of eccrine sweat gland

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002146
 * *Label* = clear cell of eccrine sweat gland
 * *Definition* = A sweat producing cell of eccrine sweat glands. Pyramidal in shape, with its base resting on the basal lamina or myoepitheliocytes, and its microvillus-covered apical plasma membrane line up the intercellular canaliculi.
