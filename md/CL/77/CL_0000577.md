
## Class : type EC enteroendocrine cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000577
 * *Label* = type EC enteroendocrine cell
 * *Definition* = A subtype of enteroendocrine cells found in the gastrointestinal mucosa, particularly in the glands of pyloric antrum; duodenum; and ileum. These cell type secretes serotonin and some neurotransmitters including enkephalins and substance P. Their secretory granules stain readily with silver (argentaffin stain).
