
## Class : preBCR-negative large pre-B-II cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000953
 * *Label* = preBCR-negative large pre-B-II cell
 * *Definition* = A pre-BCR-negative large pre-B-II cell is a large pre-B-II cell that is pre-B cell receptor-negative, composed of surrogate light chain protein (SL), which is composed of VpreB and Lambda 5/14.1, in complex with immunoglobulin mu heavy chain (IgHmu), on the cell surface, and lack a DNA rearrangement of immunoglobulin light chain genes.
