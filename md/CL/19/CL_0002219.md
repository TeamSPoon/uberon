
## Class : anchoring trophoblast

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002219
 * *Label* = anchoring trophoblast
 * *Definition* = A trophoblast found at the junction of the placenta. This cell type makes a unique fibronectin-trophouteronectin junction that helps mediate attachment of the placenta to the uterus. This cell type is also found junction of the chorion layer of the external membranes and the decidua.
