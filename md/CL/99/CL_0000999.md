
## Class : CD4-positive CD11b-positive dendritic cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000999
 * *Label* = CD4-positive CD11b-positive dendritic cell
 * *Definition* = CD8_alpha-negative CD11b-positive dendritic cell is a conventional dendritic cell that is CD11b-positive, CD4-positive and is CD205-negative and CD8_alpha-negative.
