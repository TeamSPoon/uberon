
## Class : medullary thymic epithelial cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002365
 * *Label* = medullary thymic epithelial cell
 * *Definition* = An epithelial cell of the medullary thymus. This cell type expresses a diverse range of tissue-specific antigens. This promiscuous gene expression is a cell-autonomous property of medullary epithelial cells and is maintained during the entire period of thymic T cell output.
