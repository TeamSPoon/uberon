
## Class : Bm1 B cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000961
 * *Label* = Bm1 B cell
 * *Definition* = A follicular B cell that is IgD-positive, CD23-negative, and CD38-negative. This naive cell type is activated in the extrafollicular areas through interaction with interdigitating dendritic cells and antigen-specific CD4-positive T cells.
