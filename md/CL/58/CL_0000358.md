
## Class : sphincter associated smooth muscle cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000358
 * *Label* = sphincter associated smooth muscle cell
 * *Definition* = A smooth muscle cell that is part of a sphincter. A sphincter is a typically circular muscle that normally maintains constriction of a natural body passage or orifice and which relaxes as required by normal physiological functioning.
