
## Class : primitive red blood cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002355
 * *Label* = primitive red blood cell
 * *Definition* = A large nucleated basophilic erythrocyte found in mammalian embryos. This cell type arises from the blood islands of yolk sacs and expresses different types of hemoglobins (beta-H1, gamma-1 and zeta) than adult erythrocytes. Considered a type of erythroblast as this cell type can enucleate in circulation.
