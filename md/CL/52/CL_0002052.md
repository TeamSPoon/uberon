
## Class : Fraction D precursor B cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002052
 * *Label* = Fraction D precursor B cell
 * *Definition* = A pre-B cell that is pre-BCR-negative, and the kappa- and lambda- light immunoglobulin light chain-negative, CD43-low, and is BP-1-positive, CD45R-positive and CD25-positive. This cell type is also described as being AA4-positive, IgM-negative, CD19-positive, CD43-low/negative, and HSA-positive.
