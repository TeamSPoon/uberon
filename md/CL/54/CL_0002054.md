
## Class : Fraction E immature B cell

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002054
 * *Label* = Fraction E immature B cell
 * *Definition* = An immature B cell that is IgM-positive, CD45R-positive, CD43-low, CD25-negative, and CD127-negative. This cell type has also been described as being AA4-positive, IgM-positive, CD19-positive, CD43-low/negative, and HSA-positive.
