
## Class : type I cell of carotid body

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002292
 * *Label* = type I cell of carotid body
 * *Definition* = A round or oval neuroepithelial cell that contacts other type I cells or capillaries. They occur in clusters that are surrounded by sheath cells (type-II cells) in the carotid body. This cell type is capable of secreting a number of neurotransmitters.
