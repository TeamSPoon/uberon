
## Class : metamyelocyte

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002192
 * *Label* = metamyelocyte
 * *Definition* = A eosinophil precursor in the granulocytic series, being a cell intermediate in development between a myelocyte and a band form cell. The nucleus becomes indented where the indentation is smaller than half the distance to the farthest nuclear margin; chromatin becomes coarse and clumped; specific granules predominate while primary granules are rare.
