
## Class : external supporting cell of vestibular epithelium

 * *IRI* = http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002314
 * *Label* = external supporting cell of vestibular epithelium
 * *Definition* = An auditory epithelial support cell located in the vestibular epithelium that has many hallmarks of glial cells. This cell type express glial markers such as vimentin, S100, glutamate-aspartate transporter, low affinity neurotrophin receptor p75, glial fibrillary acidic protein, and proteolipid protein.
